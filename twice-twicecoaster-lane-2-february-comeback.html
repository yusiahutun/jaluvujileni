Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, June 3, 2024

Keep your hands, arms and feet inside the vehicle at all times, because Twice is about to take us on another TWICEcoaster ride.

The nine-member South Korean troupe is returning in February with a new "special album" called TWICEcoaster: LANE 2, according to a teaser announcement on the group's Twitter yesterday (Feb. 5). Pre-orders for the album begin on Monday, February 13.

A few days earlier, the group also teased something called "KNOCK KNOCK," due to be released on February 20, which fans now assume is the release date for TWICEcoaster : LANE 2.

Following the girl group's debut in 2015 with The Story Begins, Twice have quickly become one of K-pop's hottest new exports, hitting Top 10 with their "Like Ooh-Ahh," and notching the No. 1 spot with both "Cheer Up" and the insanely infectious "TT." Their last mini-album, Twicecoaster: Lane 1, was released in October of 2016.

Prepare accordingly for the Twice takeover, Once. (Yes, that's their fan club name. See what they did there?)
