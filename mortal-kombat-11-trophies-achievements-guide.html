Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, September 27, 2024
Recommended Videos

So, as you can see, most of the trophies/achievements are easily obtainable. A quick way to get all the “perform two different fatalities” with a certain character trophy is to go into a single match against the A.I., set the difficulty to very easy, and then perform the fatalities.

The input for all the characters’ second fatality is locked in the Krypt, but you’ll have to go there, too, if you are going after all the trophies/achievements.

One trophy/achievement that might have you scratching your head is titled “Total Disrespect.” D’Vorah has an ability where, if she loses all her health in the second round, she will transform into a bug that has an entirely different move list. Use that ability, defeat the A.I. on very easy difficulty after it has defeated you, and that’s that.

Mercy returns as a “finishing move” (not really) from Mortal Kombat 3. To perform a mercy, you have to beat your opponent, wait for the FINISH HIM/HER screen to appear, go to “mid” distance, and then do the input: hold the flip stance button, press down two times and release the flip stance button. This will give your opponent a little health back. It was also used a prerequisite to perform an animality back in MK3.

Brutalities also return from MKX and work in the same way. The inputs are different for every character, and they can all be found in the Krypt.

The A.I. Battle Simulator also returns from Injustice 2. You can set the gear, augments, and behavior of your favorite fighters and let the A.I. fight some battles.

The rest of the trophies/achievements are simple. Play through the entire story, play through all the tutorials, spill a lot of blood (which you will get just by playing matches) and equip different intros and victory animations (which can be unlocked in the Krypt). Playing through and exploring the Krypt naturally will net you those last few trophies/achievements.
