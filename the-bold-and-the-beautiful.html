Did Bold &amp; Beautiful's Poppy Lie Is Luna Bill's Daughter After All?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Poppy and Luna have both been a bit of enigmas since they showed up on The Bold and the Beautiful, from Li’s seemingly outsized reaction to their showing up to the mystery of who Luna’s father is. About all we do know about him is that he’s somehow tied to a character we know

Once Bill and Poppy hooked back up, we thought for sure he would be Luna’s dad — but her dear old mom insisted that isn’t so! That’s put us back to square one, thinking that maybe Finn’s dad, Jack, might be the missing link… but that’s anything but certain. On top of that, Bill certainly hasn’t gotten the memo just yet, because we know from spoilers that he’s about to ask Poppy point blank if Luna is his daughter!

But while Poppy may be insisting Bill’s in the clear, Don Diamont isn’t at all sure where this is going. As he tells Soap Opera Digest, “Me, the actor, I have no idea what they’re doing. But I think it would be amazing! I hope that’s where it goes because I think Bill having a daughter, that dynamic would be just amazing. I just think it would be great to have to see him deal with a daughter.”

It would certainly be something new for Bill, who’s only had to deal with adult sons popping up in his life so far. Heck, it would even be a switch up for Diamont who’s got seven boys of his own! Somehow, we feel like Bill might be kind of hilariously out of his depth — at least at first!

As Diamont explains to Digest Bill would almost certainly have to figure out “a different way you have to conduct yourself when you’re pissed off about something. I could see maybe she would get emotional in a way that he hasn’t had to deal with with his sons, and I think that would be a really fun scene, sort of like the ‘There’s no crying in baseball’ scene in A League Of Their Own.”

Then, too, Wyatt and Liam would have to adjust to having a sister and, as Digest points out, there’d be one heck of a dynamic if Bill’s daughter was dating Ridge and Brooke’s son. (Not to mention working for the enemy!) Diamont enthusiastically agrees that, “There would just be a lot of story, you know? Just a lot of legs and long-term ramifications and all that. It would certainly add another layer to those relationships. I do look forward to it unfolding.”

So do we! And while we’re happy to try to get to the bottom of the mystery of who Luna’s father really is, there’s a part of us that’s really hoping Poppy lied to Luna and she’ll turn out to be another long, lost Spencer!
