Who Is Matthew Raymond, Brandon Kyle Goodman Partner? Bio, Age, Job

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Wednesday, April 24, 2024

Brandon Kyle Goodman is a nonbinary writer, actor, and advocate who has made a name for himself writing for Netflix’s animated series Big Mouth and its spin-off Human Resources. While most people are aware of Kyle’s works, his love life is relatively unknown. Today, we bring to you an article about his life partner, Matthew Raymond.

Learn about Brandon and Matthew’s relationship, Matthew’s job, age, and more in this article.

Meet Matthew Raymond, Brandon Kyle Goodman Partner

Aside from writing for Big Mouth and Human Resources, Brandon Kyle Goodman is best remembered for his role as Deco in the Netflix movie Feel the Beat. His first acting role came in the 2011 TV show Next Time on Lonny. Some of his other popular acting credits include Alive in Denver, Little Italy, Los Angeles, Curb Your Enthusiasm, Modern Love, among others. He did his undergraduate studies at NYU/Tisch School of the Arts.

Matthew and Brandon know each other since at least 2015. Matthew wrote a sweet message on Instagram for his husband on their six years of togetherness. Alongside some of their photos, Matthew wrote, “It has been 6 beautiful years of laughing, crying, growing, and learning how to thrive together… Thank you for giving me your vulnerability and for growing with me. Thank you for helping me to know I’m not too much.”

The lovebirds married in Santa Barbara, California, in 2020, just some months before the pandemic struck. On January 11, 2022, the couple celebrated their third wedding anniversary. Matthew uploaded some pictures from their marriage on the occasion and put a long but sweet caption.

Through the same IG post, Matthew revealed that his marriage with Brandon was the greatest source of healing he could have ever asked for. He added: “It has been a place where I can safely and bravely address childhood wounds, insecurities, anxieties, and internalized beliefs.”

Matthew also told about how Brandon constantly pushed and challenged him to be better and do better. In the end, Matthew appreciated his love for his kindness. He wrote, “Brandon is the kindest person I’ve ever met; his generosity knows no bounds, and the way he loves and treats everyone in his life no matter who they are as if they are the most important person on the planet melts my heart.”

The couple is smitten with each other and neither of them hesitates to appreciate each other in the public. On June 8, 2021, Matthew took to Instagram and wrote, “I’m so in love with my husband! And our love is a huge form of Pride and liberation…”

Do Matthew Raymond And Brandon Kyle Goodman Have Kids?

The lovebirds are yet to have any kids. That being said, they do own a cute dog, which constantly gets featured in their socials.

Matthew Raymond Age

Born in 1988, Matthew Raymond’s age was 33 as of this writing in 2022. He celebrates his birthday on the 18th of November.

Matthew Raymond Job

According to Matthew Raymond’s LinkedIn, he has been working as a life coach at Liberated Living since January 2021. His job as a life coach at Liberated Living is “to provide holistic coaching services for individuals who are seeking to eliminate negative thinking and get curious about their life.”

Matthew offers mainly two programs at Liberated Living. The most popular one is a mentorship program where clients have options to choose between 5 or 10 sessions packages. Since August 2021, Matthew has also been offering a 6 session program on unpacking and unlearning internalized homophobia.

Matthew also worked as a freelance DEI (Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion) consultant from July 2020 to April 2021. As a freelance DEI, he provided DEI consultation for individuals and organizations within the education or non-profit sector.

Matthew’s first job was as a 6th Grade Social Studies/Science Teacher (August 2010 – October 2012). The queer Lifecoach then worked at Peace Corps as Peace Corps Education Volunteer Leader. At Peace Corps, Matthew’s responsibility was to design and implement nationwide training and development program for volunteers and their counterparts in the field of youth leadership and development and to teach primary-age school children and primary school teachers literacy practices.

Some of Matthew’s other jobs included Outreach Director at Educators for Excellence, Student Advocate, and Mentor at Rainbow EDU Consulting and Management.

Is Matthew Raymond On Instagram?
