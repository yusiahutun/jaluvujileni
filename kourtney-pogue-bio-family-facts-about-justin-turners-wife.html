Kourtney Pogue Bio, Family, Facts About Justin Turners Wife

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 19, 2024

Watching Justin Turner play baseball, one would never imagine that this “hardened” guy is a soulmate of a beautiful “diva”. Although the baseball champion has been talked about a lot, the woman in his life – Kourtney Pogue – is not as well known. She is an actress and former Los Angeles Kings Ice Girl. The Indiana-born beauty has been in a relationship with Turner since 2012 before tying the knot in December 2017. This piece will take you through the biography, family and other fun facts about Justin Turner’s wife. Voila!

Kourtney Pogue’s bio

Kourtney Pogue was born – Kourtney ElizabethPogue – on October 31, 1986 in Chesterton, Indiana to the family of Anthony and Tina McDonald Pogue. She and her older siblings Ryan and Kristin were raised and educated in Chesterton. Although there is little information about her early childhood, we do know that Pogue attended Chesterton High School, where she graduated in 2005. After graduating from high school, she entered UCLA in 2013, where her major in psychology was to be a much-celebrated experience. For Pogue, however, it would be a bittersweet experience. Unfortunately, in February of the same year, she had lost her father. He was 67 years old at the time of his death.

Like the prettiest ladies of her type, back in school, Pogue was a cheerleader for the Indiana Ice Hockey Team and an NHL Ice Girl for the LA Kings. She also competed in the 2008 Hawaiian Week Wheel of Fortune. And had earlier contested at Miss Teen Indiana pageant. Kourtney Pogue began her career well before she graduated from college, appearing in Untold Stories from the ER (2004) and No Other High School Show (2007). She has her own business and is a self-made entrepreneur.

Pogue’s family

The Pogues were a conservative, middle-class, Christian family, although we’re not sure what Christian denomination their parents belonged to. As far as we know, Kourtney’s parents, Anthony and Tina McDonald Pogue, had three children, of which Kourtney is the youngest. She reportedly has two older siblings. a brother, Ryan Pogue and a sister, Kristin Pogue. We also have no information about her mother or what she does for a living. Thousands of Kourtney’s supporters sympathized with the Pogues when they lost their father on February 22, 2013.

Facts about Justin Turner’s wife

1. Businesswoman

Yes. Although it’s not clear what it is, Kourtney Pogue runs her own business. Talk about a woman-preneur. She operates a website www.kourtneyereppert.com. There she posts photos of herself in a bikini shot and shares details about her modeling career with visitors.

2. Fortune

With a career in modeling, film, and others, it’s safe to call Kourtney Pogue a “money maker.” While we don’t know exactly what her net worth and salary is, we do know that her husband, Justin Turner, has earned $37 million as a baseball player so far. According to a 2018 estimate, his net worth is $18 million. Turner’s break came in 2014 when he had an offensive season and earned a whopping $2.5 million in arbitration.

3. Justin Turner’s wife

As far as we know, Kourtney and Turner started dating after five years together, Turner made a spectacular proposal when he took Kourtney to the Grand Canyon on March 13, 2017, got on one knee and popped the question. They married nine months later in December 2017. Their wedding was attended by close friends and relatives in Mexico; in a ceremony culminating in a breathtaking fireworks display. So far, there hasn’t been a post from either of them that included the words “expecting” and “child” or “baby.” When news like this pops up, we’re sure you’ll hear about it first.

4. Dog lover

In November 2018, Kourtney Pogue was deeply heartbroken and soon became vengeful when her beloved miniature pinscher, Dior, was violently murdered by a neighbor’s German shepherd. According to Kourtney, the dog’s owner knew it was violent with other dogs, and as far as Dior was concerned, the owner did nothing to stop it. In addition to suing the neighbor for vet bills and other expenses associated with Dior’s death, she also sought damages for pain and suffering because she could “rely on Dior’s emotional support for her well-being.” She now has a new dog, Moonlight.

Kourtney Pogue ist anmutig schlank gebaut. Sie ist 1,73 Meter groß, hat blaue Augen und blonde Haare. Zum jetzigen Zeitpunkt sind ihre Brust-, Taillen- und Hüftmaße groß. Außerdem haben wir noch keine genauen Angaben zu ihren BH-, Schuh- und Kleidergrößen.
