Fake Fly Bait Genshin Impact

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

Genshin Impact Fake Fly Bait is one of a number of different baits you can craft in the game. In fact, it’s a great help in catching specific types of fish. That being the case, Travelers are going to want to get their hands on the Fake Fly Bait as soon as possible. However, that is a little easier said than done. There are a few hoops that you’ll have to jump through first. However, it’s nothing too difficult, as we’ll explain in this guide.

How to Get Fake Fly Bait in Genshin Impact

To get the Genshin Impact Fake Fly Bait, the first step is to get the crafting formula. To do that, find Nantuck on the shore of Cider Lake, in the east of the island Mondstadt is on. Just follow the Mondstadt Fishing Icon on the map, it’s easy. If you’re still having trouble, check out the screenshot below to see exactly where Nantuck is. In order to purchase the Fake Fly Bait formula (and any others), you’ll need three Medaka fish. For more info on that, check out our Medaka guide.

Once you have the formula, head to the nearest crafting bench to make the bait. You’ll need one Horsetail and one Sakura Bloom in order to craft it. Horsetail is a purple reed that you can find growing next to bodies of water. Alternatively, you can purchase it from Herbalist Gui in Bubu Pharmacy in Liyue Harbor. As for the Sakura Bloom Genshin Impact Fake Fly Bait ingredient, they are purple flowers that grow in Inazuma; specifically, you can find a lot of them on Narukami Island. However, you can only pick them after hitting them with an Electro attack. For more detailed info, check out our Sakura Blooms Locations and How To Get Sakura Blooms guides.

Now, after you’ve gone through all of this trouble to get the Fake Fly Bait, what exactly do you use it for? Well, according to the item’s description, they work great for catching Koi and Pufferfish and all their variants. So, if you’re on the prowl for these particular fishies, you’re gonna need this bait. Good luck!
