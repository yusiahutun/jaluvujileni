Fire Emblem: Three Houses - All Birthdays

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, September 22, 2024
Recommended Videos

Fire Emblem: Three Houses introduces an almost obscene amount of characters. As it should, it takes place in a school, after all. Beyond just the characters, the depth of the game stems from the interactions with them and possible romance. If you wish to romance someone, you’ll need to increase your Support Rank, and knowing their birthday helps immensely. You can celebrate a character’s birthday by buying them flowers for 200 gold and by inviting them to a Tea Party and correctly answering a question. All of these will increase your Support Rank with the character in question.

Nintendo Wire has a vast document that covers nearly everything one might want to know about the characters, birthdays included. It is quite long, however. If you’re looking for just the birthdays, scroll down. It’s worth noting that the months are not the same as ours, although there’s still 12 of them, so it should translate well. The calendar lists the month by number and by the moon, so both will be listed here.

  • Bernadetta – Ethereal Moon, 12/12

  • Dorothea – Horsebow Moon, 9/29
  • Ferdinand – Great Tree Moon, 4/30
  • Hubert – Great Tree Moon, 4/17
  • Linhardt – Ethereal Moon, 11/7
  • Petra – Horsebow Moon, 9/7

Blue Lions

  • Annette – Harpstring Moon, 5/9
  • Ashe – Wyvern Moon, 10/7
  • Dedue – Verdant Rain Moon, 8/31
  • Felix – Pegasus Moon, 2/20
  • Ingrid – Guardian Moon, 1/4
  • Mercedes – Harpstring Moon, 5/27
  • Sylvain – Garland Moon, 6/5

Golden Deer

  • Hilda – Pegasus Moon, 2/3
  • Ingatz – Lone Moon, 3/14
  • Leonie – Verdant Rain Moon, 8/21
  • Lorenz – Garland Moon, 6/13
  • Lysithea – Pegasus Moon, 2/28
  • Marianne – Red Wolf Moon, 11/23

Garreg Mach Monastery

Not all the characters are listed, as not every character tells the player their birthday. That said, this is all the birthdays and the characters that give them. Now woo that guy or girl of your dreams.
