Leaping Toadstool Caps for Invisibility Potion Hogwarts Legacy

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, August 26, 2024

The world of Harry Potter is filled with all sorts of fantastical flora and fauna. When it comes to plants, these are especially important, as they are ingredients in many powerful potions. For example, the Leaping Toadstool Caps is one of the ingredients you will need to find if you want to craft the Invisibility Potion in Hogwarts Legacy. We don’t have to point out just how useful invisibility can be in a game such as this, so you will definitely want to make this potion. Here’s where and how to get the Leaping Toadstool Caps.

Leaping Toadstool Caps for Invisibility Potion Hogwarts Legacy

How to Get Leaping Toadstool Caps for Invisibility Potion in Hogwarts Legacy

The Invisibility Potion consists of the following ingredients: x5 Leaping Toadstool Caps, x1 Knotgrass Sprig, and x1 Troll Bogeys. To get the recipe for this potion, go to J. Pippins Potions and buy it there. It costs 800 Galleons, while the Invisibility Potion itself costs 500 Galleons. Of course, why buy the potion when you can get the ingredients for it and make it yourselves – for free!

The Leaping Toadstool Caps themselves are very interesting and easy to spot. They look like clumps of large, red mushrooms with white spots. They are constantly wiggling in place and occasionally leaping into the air. The Leaping Toadstool Caps can be found all over the game world. Simply approach them and pick them up and you will have enough of them to craft the Invisibility Potion in no time. Speaking of the Invisibility Potion itself, it has several uses. The most obvious use is to turn invisible so that you can get a drop on enemies.

But you can also use it to open up Disillusionment Chests. When you encounter one of these, drink an Invisibility Potion and then get close to the chest itself, making sure that its eye hasn’t spotted you, and you will be able to unlock it and get the goodies stored inside.
