Lego Fortnite Steering Wheel, How to Steer Vehicles?

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, February 11, 2024

Is there a Steering Wheel in Lego Fortnite? Lego Fortnite has been a massive success since last week’s release, massing more players than all other Fortnite modes combined over the weekend. The game launched with a staggering amount of content, completely free. You can even build vehicles! In this guide, we explain how to steer a vehicle in Lego Fortnite.

Lego Fortnite Steering Wheel, How to Steer Vehicles?

Is There a Steering Wheel in Lego Fortnite?

Let’s get this straight immediately. No, in the current version of the game, there is no steering wheel in Lego Fortnite. It is a rather strange choice to omit such a tool in a game that has wheels, thrusters, and other means to build vehicles. You can create fully operational vehicles or even flying houses with balloons and thrusters. However, you will not be able to find a steering wheel.

Over the last couple of days since the game’s release, creative members of the community were able to construct everything from the most basic vehicles with just a dynamic foundation and some wheels to full-blown flying houses and moving fortresses. However, they have also learned just how hard it is to control your vehicles in Lego Fortnite because you have no steering wheel.

How to Steer Vehicles in Lego Fortnite?

So, if there are no steering wheels in Lego Fortnite, then how do you control vehicles? Well, there are alternative methods! Once again, the creative members of the community were up to the task. However, keep in mind that these methods are far from ideal and not exactly elegant solutions.

  • The first method is to use objects in your surroundings and navigate the vehicle with brute force. Allow the vehicle to crash into hard objects such as stones, ores or tree stumps, and the physics will make it turn in its direction.
  • There’s also a method where you can place one thruster, turn it on with a switch, and then place another thruster while the first one is turned on. With this method, you can simultaneously switch power between the two thrusters. For more detailed instructions on a steerable vehicle in Lego Fortnite, check out this video.
