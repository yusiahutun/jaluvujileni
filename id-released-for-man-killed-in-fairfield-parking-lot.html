ID Released For 59-Year-Old Killed In Parking Lot On Route 1 In Fairfield

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, June 26, 2024

New London County resident Stephen Brynn, of Colchester, was killed in the town of Fairfield around 9:20 a.m., Wednesday, May 17 in the parking lot of 2150 Post Road (Route 1).

Fairfield Police responded to the business after receiving a 911 call of a person struck by a vehicle, said Lt. Michael Paris of the Fairfield Police.

Upon the arrival of emergency personnel, it was determined that Brynn, who was assigned to perform work in the area as a surveyor, was hit by a vehicle in the driveway of the business, Paris said.

The driver of the vehicle was identified as a 19-year-old woman who remained at the scene and is cooperating with police, he added.

The Fairfield Police Crash Reconstruction Unit is actively investigating this incident and urges anyone with additional information to contact Officer Kevin Wells at 203-254-4859 or kwells@fairfieldct.org.
