Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub Release Date Speculations, Preview, Official A

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, February 12, 2024

Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub is eagerly awaited. Attack on Titan is one such anime that has a huge fan base. Mappa Studio has managed to retain the originality and high animation quality of AOT over all these years. But the delays in the release of dubbed episodes have surely disappointed several fans.

After a wait of several months, Attack on Titan: The Final Chapters Part 1 English Dub was released on Crunchyroll on September 11, 2023. This brings us to an important question: When will Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub be out? Read on as we discuss this in detail the same.

Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub Release Date Speculations

The release date for Part 2 hasn’t been announced yet. But we believe that it can take a good amount of time for Part 2 to be out. AOT is known for delays in dubbing. Part 1 was released after a wait of almost 6 months. In fact, many fans opined on social media that Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub should have been released with Part 1 itself. The lengthy waiting period is definitely annoying for the majority of the fans.

Attack on Titan The Final Chapters Part 2 English Dub Online Buzz

Fans have been left confused by the release schedule. Well, this confusion can be attributed to the mistake of the production committee. Even though the manga was still going on, they called the current season “The Final Season” before it was ready. This means that the next part will be the last season.
