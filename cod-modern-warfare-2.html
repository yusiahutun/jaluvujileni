COD MW2 Season 5 Ranked Playlist Removed

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 29, 2024

Not sure where is the ranked playlist in Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 Season 5? Warzone and MW2 Season 5 is finally here, bringing a slew of new and exciting content across multiplayer, battle royale and DMZ. The most exciting news about traditional multiplayer in MW2 is that we are finally getting ranked play! This guide explains where to find the Ranked Play mode in Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 Season 5.

How to Play Ranked in COD MW2

Where is Ranked Playlist in Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 Season 5?

UPDATE: COD MW2 Ranked Playlist in Season 5 will become available later today, on August 2nd, 2023. The developers confirmed that there will be a temporary downtime following the release of the new update. We will update the article once we have more details.

With the start of Season 5 in Modern Warfare 2, the long-awaited Ranked Play mode is back. Here you will be able to test your mettle and see your skill level compared to other COD players. The mode is based on the Call of Duty League. This includes everything from the 4v4 format to all official modes, maps, rules, and more. So, where is the ranked playlist in MW2? Given that it is a new addition into the game, the Ranked Play mode is currently under the “What’s Hot” tab in the main menu. Check out our featured image to see where exactly you need to lock.

However, remember that the ranked playlist is not immediately available for everyone. It goes without saying, but it is not a free mode. Rather, you will need to have MW2 purchased in order to have it available. Second, you will need to have at least Player Level 16 in order to be able to play this mode. Players below this level will have Ranked locked. Finally, it seems that there’s currently a bug which prevents some players from seeing the Ranked Mode in their menu. If that’s the case with you, try restarting the game to see if that helps. Logging in and out of your Activision account might also help fix this issue. Check out the official blog post to learn more about Ranked in MW2.
