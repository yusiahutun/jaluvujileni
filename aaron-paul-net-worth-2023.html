Aaron Paul Net Worth 2023

The two-time Emmy winner actor has proven his acting prowess with a range of great movies and shows. When he started his acting career in 2000, his breakthrough role came in 2008. Paul starred in the critically-acclaimed series Breaking Bad alongside friend and co-actor Bryan Cranston. He played the role of Jesse Pinkman for all five seasons of the series. His role in Breaking Bad earned him fame and lots of wealth. Aaron Paul’s net worth in 2023 is valued to be $35 million.

Interestingly, Paul’s character in Breaking Bad was not meant to last the whole five seasons. While talking to The Hollywood Reporter, Paul and the show’s creator Vince Gilligan revealed that Jesse Pinkman was meant to die in the first season. Gilligan stated-

“The original plan was to kill him off, but I have to say, the writers’ strike, in a sense, didn’t save him because I knew by episode 2 — we all did, all of us, our wonderful directors and our wonderful producers — everybody knew this talent, how good you are, and a pleasure to work with.”

“And it became pretty clear early on that that would be a huge, colossal mistake to kill off Jesse. But the idea was … I didn’t know how important Jesse was [going to be].”

Personal Life

Aaron Paul was born in Idaho, USA. He has been married to his wife Lauren Parsekian since 2013. The couple met during a music festival in LA and got engaged in Paris in 2012. A year later, they tied the knot in May 2013, in Calamigos Ranch, Malibu. In 2018, the couple welcomed their first child, Story Annabelle. They also had a son Ryan Caspian in 2020. They have been happily married for a decade now.

Paul’s wife, Lauren is an activist who runs a non-profit organization against bullying. Lauren was bullied as a child and hence she launched a campaign called Kind Campaign that encourages school kids to show kindness to one another. She runs these campaigns across schools with her childhood friend Molly Thompson.
