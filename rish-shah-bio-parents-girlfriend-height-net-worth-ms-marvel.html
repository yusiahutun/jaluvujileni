Meet Actor Rish Shah! His Bio, Parents, Girlfriend, Height, Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, March 23, 2024

From The Boys to the Invincible, the world had never been a better place to live in for superhero fans. And now Disney+ has another new superhero show in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, Ms. Marvel.

The Marvel Studios’ Ms. Marvel is a new, original series that introduces Kamala Khan, a Muslim American teenager growing up in Jersey City, not to mention there is a long list of other charismatic casts of young adults joining actress Iman Vellani in the lead. Among them is Rish Shah who plays Kamran, a boy Kamala has a crush on.

The rest on how Rish Shah who grew up watching Krrish and Ra. One of Bollywood landed up with the MCU show, read through this ‘Rish Shah Bio’.

Rish Shah’s Role In Ms. Marvel

Rish Shah has felt very empowering since playing Kamram in the MCU series. He said among other things, the role meant so much to him, especially as a British Indian. Hyping his role as a male lead in Ms. Marvel, in several interviews, he said it was about feeling seen. He shared that he would love watching this show even if he was not playing one of its characters. “Muslim representation has always been controversial when it comes to mainstream shows and films, but this time it felt different,” he responsibly promised.

At the same time, it was also in a way weird going from watching these people everyone idolizes as a Marvel fan to suddenly getting to meet them. When asked which superhero is he completely obsessed with from the Marvel universe, he said he loves Buck Barnes and that he is also a huge Spider-Man fan.

As for working opposite Iman, he said it was super easy and they got on so well.

Really, when Rish puts it, he says it in a way that the whole experience of this project was surreal to him, not to mention the entering the massive sets of Marvel universe part. Every time he thinks of it he is like hats off to the ability they have to pull these things together to create this world for Kamala and for them.

Rish Shah’s Net Worth

Rish Shah reportedly had less than $300K as his net worth as of 2022.

Other than Ms. Marvel, Rish has appeared in a few movies and television shows that you may recognize him from. As for TV, he was on the PrankMe, as well as Emmerdale, where he played Kirin Kotecha. He played Kirin in the soap, taking over the role from Adam Fielding, in 2020 for five episodes.

And for the films, he portrayed Ravi in the Netflix hit, To All the Boys: Always and Forever.

What’s more, Rish was most recently seen starring in Geeta Malik’s feature India Sweets and Spices which premiered at Tribeca in 2022

Rish also plays the role of Russ Lee in the upcoming Netflix feature Strangers.

Another thing you need to know is that he has completed his Bachelor’s Degree in English Language and Linguistics at King’s College London. And Rish, represented by Nicholas Gal, also received training useful for enhancing his acting skills in several workshops including the Devising Strategies with Joyce Henderson at National Youth Theatre; and one with Richard Knight.

Is Rish Shah Gay?

Rish Shah is likely not gay as there was no evidence to tell us otherwise.

Does Rish Shah Have A Girlfriend?

Rish Shah was probably single as of the time of this writing (June 2022). Or maybe wished to keep it to himself in case he had a girlfriend. And it is also like he is not going out with his girlfriends. On his social media, he could be seen hanging out separately with Maya Hawke and Camila Mendes, his costars from Strangers.

Rish Shah Age

Born in 1998, Rish Shah reached the age of 23 late in 2021.

Rish Shah Parents

Speaking to MyLondon, Rish Shah allowed the world to a story of how he went to primary school Grange Park primary which is next to Highlands School, and a secondary school called Habs in Enfield, London. He also recalled helping his parents in their shop in Enfield.

But then still, Rish chose not to tell further, the names of his parents, what brought them to the United Kingdom, what they do for a living, what it was like growing up, and all.

Is Rish Shah On Instagram?

Yes. Rish Shah could be found on Instagram @rishshah and it included 26 posts and 15.2K followers as of 10 June 2022. At the time, his other account on Twitter (@RishXShah) was seemingly removed.

Rish Shah Height

Very handsome tall, dark, and handsome Rish Shah when being honored to be featured as “Mr. Vogue” for British Vogue in January 2022, stood 6 feet tall in height.
