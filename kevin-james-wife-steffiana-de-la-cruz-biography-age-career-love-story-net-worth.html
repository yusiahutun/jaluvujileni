Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 29, 2024

Filipino-American actress Steffiana de la Cruz has showcased her exceptional skills in the films Paul Blart: Mall Cop and Zookeeper. She is widely recognized as the spouse of actor Kevin James, who played the lead role of ‘Doug’ in the popular American sitcom The King of Queens.

Early Life

Born in the USA on August 28, 1974, the Filipino-American actress was not only stunning but also gifted. At birth, she was given the name Stephanie Anna Marie Zantua.

Steffiana is a member of the Filipino minority and holds American citizenship. However, there is a lack of information about her educational background and accomplishments. As a teenager, her family relocated to New York.

Career

The focus of Steffiana de la Cruz‘s profession has largely centered on the entertainment industry. She is an exceptional performer and model in numerous successful films. Although she initially started as a model, she eventually recognized various chances to pursue acting, in which she thrived.

Her first television appearance was in 1997 as a masseur in the episode “Das Duell” of the German crime series Die Gang. Following this, she seized the chance to make an appearance on the CBS show The King of Queens.

Afterward, Steffiana de la Cruz landed a role as a masseuse in season 4 of Eddie Money. She then made appearances as Tine in season 6 of Damned Yanky, as Sue in season 7 of The Blacklist, and as Sienna in season 9 of The Affair Trade.

In Zookeeper, she portrayed the character of Robin, who was Kevin James’ wife. She also took on the role of Kira in the 2009 sequel Paul Blart: Mall Cop 2.

Married Life

Steffiana de la Cruz and actor Kevin James have been happily married for 16 years and have four children. They tied the knot when James was 39 and Steffiana was 30 years old.

Before dating Kevin, Steffiana was romantically involved with Chris Penn from 1993 to 1999 until his passing. Penn, an American actor, was recognized for his performances in multiple movies, including The Wild Life, The Funeral, Corky Romano, and Rush Hour.

In 2001, Steffiana de la Cruz was set up on a blind date with Kevin James by his interior designer. The two hit it off and their relationship progressed quickly. They became engaged in 2003 and tied the knot the following year in Dana Point, California.

Their close friends and Kevin James’ family members were present as they exchanged their vows and their special day was graced by their loved ones. The Montage Resort in Laguna Beach was the venue for their wedding reception ceremony.

The couple has been blessed with a family of four children. Their oldest daughter, Sienna-Marie James, was born in September 2005 and is now 17 years old. Their second child, Shae Joel, was born in June 2007. Sistina Sabella James, their third child, was born in 2015, and their youngest, Kannon Valentine James, was born in 2011.

Steffiana de la Cruz’s Net Worth

Steffiana is a well-off actress and model who has accumulated a fortune of around $5 million through her work in show business. In comparison, her famous partner Kevin James has a net worth of $85 million, primarily from his successful acting career.

Steffiana de la Cruz and her husband, Kevin James, have created a wonderful life together for their children. Despite her personal and professional achievements, she remains predominantly recognized as Kevin James’ spouse.
