Berks Woman Embezzled $51K From School PTO: Troopers

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, May 21, 2024

Alison Haines of Shoemakersville is charged with felony theft and access device fraud and surrendered to authorities on Tuesday, Sept. 26, according to troopers. 

Investigators said she made $51,492 in unauthorized transactions using a business debit card issued to her by the Perry Elementary School PTO. 

She spent the money at Aldi's, Walmart, and Amazon.com, and also made "a significant amount" of ATM withdrawals, state police said. 

Haines, 39, was released on $7,500 bail and is due back in court for her preliminary hearing on Oct. 30, court records show.
