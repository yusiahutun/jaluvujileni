Tractor Trailer Erupts In Flames On Route 78

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, May 13, 2024

At 10:39 p.m. the Whitehouse Fire Company and Oldwick Fire Company responded to Route 78 eastbound by exit 78 for a tractor trailer fire, the Whitehouse Fire Company said on Facebook.

Firefighters used multiple lines to attack the blaze and were able to declare the fire under control before midnight, firefighters said.
