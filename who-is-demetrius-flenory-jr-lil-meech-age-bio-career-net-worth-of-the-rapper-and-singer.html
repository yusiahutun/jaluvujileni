Who Is Demetrius Flenory Jr. Lil Meech? Age, Bio, Career, Net Worth Of The Rapper And Singer

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, January 28, 2024

Demetrius Flenory Jr. also known as Lil Meech is an American singer, rapper, and actor. He is the son of Demetrius Flenory Sr., popularly known as Big Meech, an American drug trafficker, money launderer, and tax evader. Despite the fact that his father has been found guilty of money laundering and drug trafficking, Demetrius Flenory Jr. is not involved in his father’s illegal activities.

Who Is Demetrius Flenory Jr.?

Demetrius Flenory Jr. was born to notorious drug boss Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory on April 22, 2000, in Detroit, USA. He was enrolled in a private school in his hometown. After his schooling, he was enrolled in the University of Nevada, Las Vegas. Lil Meech’s uncle, Terry P.O. Lee Flenory also works with his father. Demetrius Flenory Jr. has a towering build, curly hair, brownish-black eyes, and long hair.

More About Demetrius Flenory Jr.

50 Cent’s Starz series Black Mafia Family includes Demetrius Flenory Jr. The Black Mafia Family series chronicles the rise to prominence of his father and uncle in the early 2000s criminal underworld. Due to his father’s restricted freedom and privileges as a prisoner, his absence created a void in the movie industry, and Demetrius Jr. was the only actor who could take on the role of Big Meech due to the lack of other actors who had his likeness.

Curtis “50 Cents” Jackson took Demetrius to an acting class to help him get ready for the part, spurred by the circumstances. Even if it was for a good cause, Demetrius “Big Meech” Flenory Sr. noted that the entire exercise was exhausting and depriving; he considered it intense because he attended lessons twice a day, five days a week, for two years.

Demetrius’s fame reached new heights after the series’ success when he starred in the Netflix original series “Euphoria”. Due to his popularity, he now has over a million Instagram followers under the handle @lilmeechbmf.

Lil Meech is still making waves in the entertainment industry, and his representation of his father’s heritage in “Black Mafia Family” is just one more example of how talented and dedicated he is to telling gripping stories on screen. His career in show business is intricately connected to his family’s past, providing an engrossing story that has drawn the interest of both reviewers and fans.

The well-known mobster, Big Meech is scheduled to be released from prison in 2028. He hasn’t spent a lot of time interacting with his kids as a result. He has not had a typical relationship with his children because he is incarcerated and cannot communicate with the outside world. After he is released from prison, things could not be the same. When Lil Meech’s father was sentenced to 30 years in federal prison, he was only five years old. It’s interesting to note that he maintained a close relationship and continuous communication with his father during the BMF TV series development.
