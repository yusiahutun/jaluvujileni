Everything We Know About Toby Keith Daughter Krystal Keith

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

The late Toby Keith was a legendary figure in the musical industry. So, Let’s get a chance to know about his personal life and one of his children.

Today, we will discuss the personal and professional life of Toby Keith daughter Krystal Keith. Let’s find out what the daughter of the late singer is up to these days.

A Brief Overview About the Late Country Singer

  • The late singer was born on July 8, 1961, in Clinton, Oklahoma, United States of America. His full name was Toby Keith Covel, and his parents were Carolyn Joan and Hubert K. Covel Jr.
  • He had a brother and a sister. Keith’s family moved to Moore, Oklahoma, when he was still a small child, after spending a few years in Fort Smith, Arkansas, while he was in grade school.
  • Toby attended Highland West Junior High and Moore High School, where he played defensive end for the football team after the family relocated to Moore.
  • Keith moved to Nashville, Tennessee, and tried his luck busking along Music Row. After hearing a copy of Keith’s demo tape, producer Harold Shedd signed him to Mercury Records.
  • From 1979 to 1980, Covel attended Villanova University, where he received an honorary degree. He intended to work as a petroleum engineer.

Meet Toby Keith Daughter Krystal Keith?

Toby was married to Tricia Lucus from 1984 to 2024 until his death. From their marriage, they had two biological children. One of them is Krystal Keith. She was born in Norman, Oklahoma, on September 30, 1985.

Kriysal attended the University of Oklahoma and obtained an undergraduate degree. Similarly, she followed in her father’s footsteps and entered the musical industry in 2004. She sang duet vocals with Toby on the cover of Inez Foxx and Charlie Foxx‘s Mockingbird.

The father and daughter duo sang the song at the 2004 Country Music Association Awards. Keith went to college at her father’s persuasion following the popularity of this song.

Career Highlights of the American Singer

After beginning her career in 2004, Toby Keith daughter Krystal Keith showed no signs of stopping. As soon as she completed her degree, she quickly started writing songs.

The American national secured a contract with Show Dog-Universal Music in mid-2013. The first song on her four-song extended play, “Daddy Dance with Me,” was released in the same year.

Similarly, on her wedding day, Krystal gave her father a surprise performance of the song she had written. Her father and Mark Wright produced her debut album, Whiskey & Lace, which features multiple songs.

Daryl Addison of Great American Country gave the EP a favorable review, describing the artist as a “confident young singer with a strong voice.” On September 30, 2013, Whiskey & Lace’s second song, “Get Your Redneck On,” was made available to country radio.

Meet Krystal’s Husband – A Look Into Her Personal Life

Like her late father, the American country singer also found success in the musical industry. Therefore, fans want to know the relationship status of Krystal. So, who is she married to?

Krystal is happily married to Andrew Robert Sandubrae. They have kept the details regarding their first meet-up a secret from the public.

However, we do know that Mr. and Mrs.Sandubrae went backpacking across Europe in 2009 and traveled to nine countries and thirteen cities. Thus, it is evident that they have been together for over fifteen years.

After dating for a couple of years, Krystal and Andrew got married on October 23, 2010, in front of their friends, family, and well-wishers.

Similarly, the lovely couple’s family became bigger when Krystal gave birth to their first daughter in 2016. Her name is Hensley Sandubrae. Further, the pair’s second daughter, Kirby Kaye Sandubrae, was born in 2019.

The happy family of four can be constantly seen celebrating holidays and going on vacations. We wish them all the world’s happiness and hope their bond grows stronger with time.
