Get To Know TikTok Star Victoria Paris Net Worth, Parents, Dating

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Thursday, February 15, 2024

Victoria Paris thought it was the end of the world when her boyfriend cheated on her. But little did she know, that event would lead her to be a TikTok star, discover her sexuality, and later, land a girlfriend hotter than any guy she’s ever been with.

This article contains everything you need to know about her.

Who Is TikTok Star Victoria Paris?

Victoria Paris rose to the limelight in 2020 after using her TikTok to advertise her Depop store. Quickly garnering millions of fans — The “Victorians,” as her fans call themselves — Victoria then started posting herself getting dressed, working out, injecting Botox, and sometimes ranting about “cancel culture,” which earned her even more fans.

So, what makes Victoria different than other TikTokers who broadcast everything they do? Well, she’s an accidental star. Unlike many clout chasers, Victoria never wanted to be an influencer. Thus, if being on TikTok hindered her everyday life she wouldn’t hesitate to quit it all. In her words, “I think this sh*t is so f**king dumb. At the end of the day, I want to be free. And if this is a means to an end, then so be it. I’ll be the best influencer I can be.

TikTok Star Victoria Paris Net Worth

Victoria Paris flaunted a net worth of above $300 thousand in 2022, all thanks to her time on social media.

“Social media is my work and my play,” she said. As a content creator, she worked 9 – 10 hours a day, even though she disliked using a laptop. No wonder, she was able to turn her vlog-like content turned into brand deals and ads while still adhering to her likable relatability.

Her content ranged from fashion to fitness or sharing whatever was on her mind.

Branded as “the only living girl,” Victoria worked as a marketing manager before starting TikTok. Sadly, due to the pandemic, she was laid off in March 2020.

Yes, life wasn’t easy for her. Infact, as a child, she sold homemade creations to Chapel Hill’s local comic store. In high school, she was arrested for racing her car. But thanks to it, she learned the business of thrifting while completing community service at a Christian thrift store.

Later, Victoria even got into alcohol and drugs and she was flipping clothes at Indiana University to pay for them.

Now, life’s better. She has over a million followers and tons of brand deals. Surprisingly, Urban Outfitters even covered her new place, and much of her apartment was furnished by Anthropologie via a partnership

Fun Fact: Someday, Victoria wants to make it to Forbes’ 30 Under 30.

TikTok Star Victoria Paris Parents

Victoria Paris has been eclectic and dreamed of super-lived-in space from the start, but she has her parents; especially her mom to thank for her style.

According to the TikTok star, her mother would send her to elementary school in a jeans jacket with no top underneath (full nipples out kinda thing). “She would always tell me that my style is funky and I should be proud of it,” Victoria recalled.

Fast-forward to 2022, her style is still the same. “I think style and fashion can definitely be innate but you may not have the resources to express it the way you want,” she added.

Also, Victoria shared that it was her mother who taught her to cut off her friends who came to her only when they need something.

As for her siblings, Victoria has a brother who worked in finance.

Is TikTok Star Victoria Paris Dating Anyone?

Yes, as of 2022, Victoria Paris is dating her girlfriend who prefers to stay anonymous. But even though Victoria didn’t share her lover’s name, she did mention that her girlfriend wasn’t popular.

The two first connected through mutual friends on the social media application BeReal. Victoria then flew to LA to see her and it was then and there that she knew her date was “older, not on TikTok, is a scientist, and has a Ph.D.”

One day (in early 2022), Victoria was then proposed to by her girlfriend at Cafe Gitane in Nolita. Recalling the moment, Victoria shared her girlfriend wore a -shirt with the words “my friend” scrawled suspiciously near her armpit. And as she said, “My friend? That’s nice. I like the color,” her girlfriend pulled the sleeve out of her and it said, “Will you be my girlfriend?”

In late 2022, there was speculation that Nico @nicoandy was Victoria’s girlfriend.

Talking about Victoria’s sexuality, she only discover she was bisexual in 2021. Then, she dated a girl who “sucked the soul” out of her. And in 2022, she met the love of her life.

Now, Victoria believes “nobody will love a woman the way another woman loves a woman because they see you for who you are and all the intricacies that men don’t see.”
