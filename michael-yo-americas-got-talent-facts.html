America's Got Talent Michael Yo

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 2, 2024

The last day of June 2020 in America's Got Talent brought several talents, including from never heard genre (in AGT) poetry to others. And among them, one was Michael Yo, an American TV show host and a comedian, who blew the mind of the whole judge panel.

His comedic performance during the audition in America's Got Talent received numerous compliments that Yo broke into tears as he went on being emotional. Eventually, he won four 'Yes' and entered the next round of the widely celebrated NBC's franchise show, America's Got Talent.

You will need to see some background of one of the most talented contestants of the AGT 2020. See Below!!

• Michael Yo Nearly Died Of Coronavirus (COVID-19)

Micheal Yo got diagnosed with the novel Coronavirus (COVID-19) and pneumonia at the same time, which made his health degrade hour by hour. In an emotional video on Instagram, Michael revealed his health crisis.

Later, he underwent the strict supervision of doctors and medical personnel in the ICU (Intensive Care Unit) for eight days.

Michael Yo in ICU (©: Talenrecap)

After the recovery, Yo appeared on The Wendy Williams Show and told that he was better. He said:

"I'm about 85 percent better. But at times, like right now, I still have to think to kind of catch my breath."

His doctors believed that he would not make it through the disease; however, he is still alive to this date.

• He Is A Married Man And Has Children

Michael Yo is married for three years, as of 2020, with his wife, Claire Elise Schreiner. The lovebirds dated for a year and tied their nuptial knot on 14 February 2017, on the occasion of Valentine's Day.

His wife, Claire Elise is an American athlete, who is a four-time state gymnastics champion at Campbell County High School in Gillette, Wyoming. She represented Wyoming in the Miss USA 2010 pageant. Later, in 2017, she competed on the survivalist game show Kicking & Screaming.

Michael Yo and his wife Claire Elise Yo at their marriage (©: Instagram/Claire Elise Yo)

Michael Yo and Claire Elise are parents of their two children Oliver Yo and Elise. Currently, the love duo resides in Los Angeles with their happy family.

• Michael Yo Has A Close-Knit Family

The 45-year-old comedian is a self-proclaimed 'Half-Black Brother with a Korean Mother.' His parents raised him in Houston, and currently, he lives in Los Angeles with his family.

 

Michael Yo with his parents (©: Instagram/Michael Yo)

He belongs to the mixed ethnicity as his father, Oliver is an Afro-American while his mother, Kum, is a Korean. He often calls his family' Blassians,' who are always supportive of every positive step of his life.

• Michael Yo's Net Worth Ranges In Thousand

Michael Yo has made his prominence as a TV show host and a comedian as well. Through his satisfactory career progressions, he has made a net worth $300 Thousand.

As being one of the active members of the entertainment world, he starred in several productions like E! Presents Comcast on Demand (2003), The Cookout 2 (2011), The Bling Ring (2013), Kevin Can Wait (2017-18), Happy Together (2019), and many others.

As of 2020, he hosts CBS television series The Insider and the Yo Show on Yahoo! TV. Similarly, he was the one who coached the Khloe and Kourtney Kardashian as radio DJs.
