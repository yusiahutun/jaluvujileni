General Hospital Spoilers April 29, 2024 Through May 3, 2024

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Friday, July 19, 2024

Soaps.com has the latest, new General Hospital spoilers from Monday, April 29, to Friday, May 3. Anna continues to pursue a truth she may not really want to face, while Sonny issues a warning regarding Dex. Elsewhere, Josslyn turns to a friend when she needs to confide in someone, and Drew seems poised to kick off a major new project. Read on for all the details!

General Hospital spoilers week of April 29:

General Hospital spoilers for Monday, April 29:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Ava tells Nikolas about Sonny’s meds, Kristina confirms Dex was ordered to kill Cyrus, and Nina and Drew have sex again.

We may finally get a bit more insight into what’s really going on with Ava when she pays Nikolas a visit and choose to confide in him.

Anna hopes Jason may help her uncover the truth about Valentin. But will she ultimately wind up regretting this decision?

Willow is about to receive an offer which could prove life-changing.

John mixes it up with Jason.

Nina may find herself giving in to temptation!

General Hospital spoilers for Tuesday April 30:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Anna realizes Valentin has been lying all along to her about Pikeman, Sasha and Cody share a kiss, and Nina is surprised when Willow decides to give her another chance.

Will Dex answer honestly when he’s put in the hot seat by Jason? Should be interesting to watch two of Sonny’s former employees interact!

Natalia isn’t exactly one to bite her tongue, so she’s ready to tell Maxie everything that’s on her mind regarding Blaze and her role at Deception!

Sasha needs comfort, and she knows that Cody will be able to provide it for her.

Drew and Nina continue to walk the treacherous line that exists between them when he asks a favor of her.

Valentin has a proposition for Anna.

General Hospital spoilers for Wednesday, May 1:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Elizabeth worries Aiden’s being bullied again, and Natalia makes a bad impression on Alexis.

Nina hasn’t had the best run of luck lately, but she’s feeling both hopeful and optimistic today!

So far, we’ve seen Natalia deal with Sonny quite a bit. But today, she’s going to pay Kristina’s other parent, Alexis, a visit!

TJ continues to think he and Molly made a mistake in asking Kristina to be their surrogate. Could Kristina lose TJ and Molly’s baby?

Thinking quickly, Maxie comes up with one of her famous — if not necessarily foolproof — plans.

Why is Elizabeth having to fret about Aiden?

General Hospital spoilers for Thursday, May 2:

In today’s General Hospital recap, Maxie and Lucy agree on who should be Deception’s new spokesmodel, Tracy tests Sasha’s cooking abilities, and Willow makes a decision about her future.

Does the fact that Willow is suddenly questioning her career choices have anything to do with the chat she and Drew had earlier this week?

Cody and Sasha finally decide it’s time to have a cards-on-the-table kind of conversation about their budding relationship.

Will Tracy be open to the advice Gregory offers?

Lois is not only in daughter Brook Lynn’s corner, but steps up to the plate in order to coach her!

General Hospital spoilers for Friday, May 3:

Sonny is clearly still feeling betrayed by Dex, which would explain why he warns son Dante about the guy.

Carly comes to a realization regarding Sonny. (Might she be the person who finally figures out what’s really going on with him?)

Concerns about Kristina are weighing heavily upon several members of her family.

To whom is Drew about to pitch a brand new business project?

Josslyn takes Trina into her confidence.
