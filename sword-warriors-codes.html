How to Redeem Sword Warriors Codes

To redeem the Roblox codes in Sword Warriors in January 2024 (and presumably in the future), you just have to go through a few relatively simple steps. It’s not that different from most other games. First off, open the game on your preferred device. Next up, click or tap the Options button on the left side of the screen. This will open a new menu. At the bottom of it, there’s a text box in which you can enter one of the codes from the list above. To complete the process, just tap the green Accept button next to the box. Rinse and repeat until you’ve used up every code you wanted.
