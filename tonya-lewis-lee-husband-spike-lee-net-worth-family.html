Tonya Lewis Lee Relationship With Husband Spike Lee, Net Worth Insight, &amp; Family

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 1, 0001

Tonya Lewis Lee is an American advocate who assists women and newborn infants for their well-being. In 1998, she opened her organization, Madstone Company Inc. After she opened her agency, she distributed kid's books alongside making content for the Nickelodeon channel.

Apart from being an advocate, she is also a movie producer, business visionary, and author. From 2007 to 2013, she served as the representative for A Healthy Baby Begins With You raising campaign for infant mortality awareness, which took place at the U.S. Branch of Health and Human Service Office.

Tonya Lewis Lee Relationship With Husband Spike Lee

Tonya Lewis Lee, a 29-year-old producer, is married to an American filmmaker and professor, Spike Lee, since 1992.

The couple met at a Congressional Black Caucus dinner and began their relationship in 1992. After dating for a year, the couple tied a knot in New York on 2 October 1993.

How Much Is Tonya Lewis Lee?

Tonya Lewis Lee is an American filmmaker and business entrepreneur whose net worth is yet to get surfaced. Meanwhile, her husband, Spike Lee, cherishes a net worth of $50 million.

Through Spike Lee, she was introduced to the world of entertainment and glamour during the 1990s.

She started her career by working as the content creator for the Nickelodeon channel. Afterward, she ventured with the channel for large project schemes such as Miracle's Boys (2005) and That's What I'm Talking About (2006). 

In 2014, she teamed up with Nikki Silver to produce a company, Tonik Productions. The company produces content for several projects such as The Watsons Go To Birmingham (2013), Queen Bee (2014), and She's Gotta Have It (2017).

Besides being a producer, Lewis is also an essayist who has written many kids related books such as Please Baby Please, Please Puppy Please, and Giant Steps to Change the World. Likewise, she is the co-writer of the New York Times bestselling Gotham Diaries and the author of the content, The Watsons Go to Birmingham.

In 2015, she teamed up with Robert Sires to locate Movta Organics, a site for selling organic multi-vitamins to women, movitaorganics.com.

Tonya Lewis Lee Early Life, & Family

Tonya Lewis Lee was born on March 15, 1966, in Yonkers, New York. She is the daughter of George Ralph Lewis and Lillian Charlotte, who came from North Virginia.

Her father, George, worked as a financial executive while her mother, Lillian, worked as a high school biology teacher. In the family, Tonya has a sister named Tracey Lewis Elligan.

She studied and graduated from Sarah Lawrence College with a BA. Afterward, she studied at the University of Virginia School of Law and graduated with a JD.
