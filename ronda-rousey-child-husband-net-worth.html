UFC Fighter Ronda Rousey Welcomes First Child With Husband Travis Browne| Insight About Their Marrie

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, May 27, 2024

Ronda, 34, had married her husband, Travis Browne, in August 2017 and announced that they were expecting a new member in a youtube video breaking their pregnancy news back in April. She said, “I can’t hide it anymore . So it’s time to show-it off.”

The pro wrestler Ronda Rousey has become a mother of a baby girl for the first time and revealed the exciting news in a sweet social media posted on 27 September.

The athlete shared a couple of pics with the tiny newborn hugging her mum’s chest with only a tiny hand seen. The post was captioned, “Welcome to this world La’akea Makalapuaokalanipo Browne. You are so incredibly loved.”

The WWE star couple had shared that they were interested in having kids but weren’t in any rush to grow their family, saying, “We’re just putting it out in the universe and letting nature take its course and not trying to rush it or put any pressure on ourselves. Just kind of enjoy our time as a family together . Enjoying everyday and accepting it whenever it happens.”

Though it’s her first child, She is already a mother, a stepmother to two children of her husband’s children from his past relationships.

The couple announced their baby’s gender via Rousey’s Youtube channel in an anime style. They decided to straightforwardly reveal the gender of their baby in a unique way. She explained, “We didn’t want to do anything that would set hundreds of acres ablaze, or thousands even.Some people are idiots. We’re keeping it simple , we’re keeping it safe, we’re keeping it Browsey Acres style.”

They revealed the gender by showing a green spotted egg hatching to reveal a sonogram photo with a text that said, “ It’s a baby girl” underneath.

Ronda Rousey Relationship Details With Husband Travis Browne

The Couple Are A Perfect Match

Ronda Rousey met her husband in a match back in 2015 and started dating fellow MMA fighter, Travis Browne. And after two years, she exchanged vows and tied the knot with him in a beautiful ceremony held in Hawaii. The couple is perfectly matching with each other as their career and achievements align with each other and show how strong their bond is.

While Ronda was the first female to sign with UFC, her husband Travis was also a formidable opponent in the UFC octagon. Her husband is very supportive of her wrestling career and even teased the idea of going to ring with her someday by saying, “I’ll never say no to the possibility of going out there and wrestling.” He added. “ I’d love to go out there and work with my wife.”

Ronda’s husband, Travis Browne, is an American mixed martial athlete who was ranked number 5 in official UFC heavyweight rankings. Travis has not had a match since 2017, making his last win in the 2016 match with Matt Mitrione. He had been married to Erin Browne in 2001 and got separated in 2009. Then he was married to Jenna Renee Webb and got into an affair with Ronda while still being in a divorce process. With his first wife, Travis became a father to two sons named Kaleo and Keawe.

Net Worth Of Ronda Rousey

Ronda Rousey is an American mixed martial artist who is also an actor. She is an American MMA competitor who has a net worth of $13 million.

Ronda Rousey was the youngest American to earn the national No. 1 ranking in the women’s half-middleweight division. She was also the first American woman in 12 years to earn a World Championship medal by finishing second in the 2007 tournament. She retired from judo at the age of 21 and then joined the Glendale fighting club to make her debut in mixed martial arts by 2010.

Eventually, Rousey became the Strikeforce Women’s Bantamweight Champion by defeating Miesha Tate in four minutes and 27 seconds. She was also featured on a cover of ESPN the magazine's 2012 Body issue and had appeared as a guest on Conan O’Brien’s talk show.

Rousey was also the first woman to sign with the world’s largest mixed martial arts league, UFC. She had won four consecutive round victories when she had returned to the UFC Octagon.  Then she was knocked out by Holly Holm in November 2015 and was in athletics challenge since her days of competitive judo. However, she came back with better spirits and was told she was competitive and loved to do what she set out to do by the president of UFC, Dana White. Though she lost to Amanda Nunes in the main event of UFC 207, she had professional records of 12 wins and two losses.

Rousey had appeared in The Expendables 3(2014), where she portrayed a nightclub bouncer recruited to join a team of mercenaries. She had also appeared in Furious 7 and Entourage, both films in 2015. Then in 2018, she starred in the international crime thriller Mile 22, and the following summer, she was announced to join season 3 of the procedural drama series 9-1-1, where she would play the role of a member of the Los Angeles Fire Department.

Rousey made her debut at WWE in 2018 and had won the Raw Women’s Championship by defeating Alexa Bliss and held the title until she lost to Becky Lynch at Wrestlemania in 2019. When she was on a break with a broken hand as an injury, she announced that she wanted to focus on starting a family with her husband and later revealed that she had no interest in returning to WWE on a full-time basis.
