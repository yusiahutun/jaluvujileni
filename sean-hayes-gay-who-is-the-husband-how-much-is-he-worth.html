Sean Hayes Gay, who is the husband, how much is he worth?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Will & Grace star Sean Hayes is a multi-talented entertainer. He is an actor, singer, producer and comedian. Born in Chicago on June 26, 1970, Irish-born Hayes decided to act at an early age. His first movie role was as an extra in the movie Lucas (1986) which was shot at his high school, Glenbard West High in Glen Ellyn, Chicago. After studying piano practice at Illinois State, Hayes performed as a classical pianist before moving into improv comedy and then stand-up comedy. In order to improve his chances of succeeding in the entertainment industry, Hayes moved to Los Angeles in 1995 and two years later hit big with his role as Jack McFarland,

For his role as Jack McFarland, Sean received seven consecutive Emmy Award nominations for Major Actor in a Comedy Series, earning his first nomination in 2000. He was also nominated five consecutive times for the Golden Globe Award for Best Actor – Series , Miniseries or TV Movies. Sean Hayes has won three SAG Awards for his role and an American Comedy Award.

How much is Sean Hayes worth?

Much like star actors from ace TV shows like Friends, Modern Family, Big Bang Theory, etc., Sean Hayes got most of his TV fortune from Will & Grace. Like the aforementioned TV shows, Will & Grace stars, Sean Hayes included, often negotiate a pay rise based on the show’s popularity. In 1998, he would earn $400,000 per episode of the series.

His numbers skyrocketed and hit his peak in 2006, when Hayes earned $600,000 per episode. The show ended this year after running for 8 long seasons. Much to the delight of fans and of course Sean’s bank account, the show was revived in 2017, resuming the same as it did in Season 8.

For the revival, Hayes, who in 2004 made the smart move from his production company, Hazy Mills Productions, took on the dual roles of actor and executive producer. In the first season of the revived series, namely season 9, Sean Hayes and the other main actors of the series earned $250,000 per episode. Hayes’ numbers were certain to increase if the show’s revival had what it takes to excite its fans like it originally did.

During the Will & Grace period, Sean Hayes became involved in other jobs. He appeared in films like April Pieces (2003) and Win a Date with Tad Hamilton! (2004). On the small screen, he appeared on Scrubs, Martin and Lewis and hosted an episode of Saturday Night Live.

After Will & Grace first wrapped in 2006, Hayes kept busy with his production company. He became the executive producer of shows such as Hot in Cleveland, Grimm, The Soul Man, Sean Saves the World. One of Hayes’ most successful works at his production company is the Hollywood Game Night game show he created.

Hayes is also known for his many voice roles. In recent years, he has voiced characters in Monsters University, How Murray Saved Christmas, and The Emoji Movie

Sean Hayes net worth stands at $18 million. Some of his assets include a 4-bedroom house in Hancock Park, Los Angeles, which he purchased in 2001 for $1.6 million.

Is he gay, who is the husband?

In November 2014, Sean Hayes married his partner of 8 years, a certain Scott Icenogle, in a private ceremony organized during the Thanksgiving holiday. Scott is a composer.
