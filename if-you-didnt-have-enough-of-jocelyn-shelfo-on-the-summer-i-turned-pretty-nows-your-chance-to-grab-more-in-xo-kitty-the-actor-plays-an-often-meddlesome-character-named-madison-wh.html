Get To Know Jocelyn Shelfo! Her Age, Parents, Boyfriend, XO, Kitty

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, March 1, 2024

If you didn’t have enough of Jocelyn Shelfo on The Summer I Turned Pretty, now’s your chance to grab more. In XO, Kitty, the actor plays an often meddlesome character named Madison, who is an American obsessed with K-pop and Korean culture to a cringy extent.

Below, you’ll learn all about Jocelyn’s age, parents, boyfriend, net worth, and more.

Jocelyn Shelfo On XO, Kitty

To All the Boys spin-off, XO, Kitty premiered on Netflix on May 18, 2023, and it followed Lara Jean Covey’s youngest sister Kitty, who headed off to study in Seoul, South Korea. Fortunately, she wasn’t the only foreigner in the country as Madison,  an American obsessed with K-pop and Korean culture — portrayed by Jocelyn — resided in Korea.

Moreover, Madison’s father was a business associate of Yuri’s dad. So, they were already acquainted.

Recalling the filming process, Jocelyn said that it was such a “special experience” that led her to so many amazing people. “I will forever be grateful. So much love to everyone who was a part of it,” she said.

Also, after gracing the XO, Kitty premiere stage, she took to her IG to write, “What a wonderful night celebrating everyone’s hard work!! Can’t wait for everyone to see the show (May 18th) And thanks to these special people for making me feel pretty ;)”

How Much Is Jocelyn Shelfo Net Worth?

Jocelyn Shelfo garnered a net worth of under $200 thousand by 2023.

 XO, Kitty was Jocelyn’s biggest role after she appeared as “Marisa” in The Summer I Turned Pretty in 2022. Prior to that, she played “Stevie” in the short movie Greener Pastures (2022), “Liz” in the short film The Great Princess Diana Caper (2022), and “Charlie” in another short movie Framed (2020). Also, she had a short-lived role as a receptionist in America Soul (2020), and “Marilee Manley” in Your Worst Nightmare (2017).

But though Jocelyn now has the international spotlight on her, she’s yet to graduate from Savannah College of Art and Design. If everything goes smoothly, Jocelyn will get her Performing Arts degree in 2024.

However, with that benign said, Jocelyn certainly has had multiple training from experts like  Lea Patrick, Cheryl Harris, Charlie Winfield, Heather Finn,  Ben Yang, Craig Holzberg, and  Ken Buswell in Entertainment Management, Audition Techniques, TV Commercial Acting, Monologue Techniques, Improvisational Acting, Photo Movement and Runway, and more.

The last we checked, Jocelyn was dinged to Evolution Talent Agency, the same agency that represented the child actor Kobe Wilder.

When Jocelyn wasn’t acting, she loved to travel, play the violin, and water ski/skate. Also, she’s interested in Sprinting, Basketball, and Baseball.

Jocelyn Shelfo Age

Jocelyn Shelfo was 22 years of when she filmed in XO, Kitty. However, she could be a year older by the time you see her on the show in 2023.

Who Are Jocelyn Shelfo Parents?

Jocelyn Shelfo was born to her parents, Scott and Christine Shelfo.

Her father, Scott is a Lindenhurst Senior High School, Albany Medical College, and  Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute graduate who worked at Georgia Urology. He turned 56 in Dec 2022.

As for her mother, Christine only turned 56 in May 2023. Find Christine on FB @chrisitne.shelfo

Talking about her siblings, Jocelyn has a brother named Gavin and a sister named Lauren Shelfo.

Gavin is a track and field runner at Middle Tennessee State University. He was set to graduate in 2026.

On the other hand, Lauren received her Bachelor’s degree in Aviation Flight with Commercial Concentration and a double minor in Aviation Management & Spanish Bachelor’s degree from California Baptist University in May 2022. Now, she works as an Academic Aviation Tutor at her alma mater. “I hold weekly tutoring sessions for all students in the aviation program at California Baptist University. We review any and all ground and flight knowledge for flight students,” she said.

Sadly, Jocelyn’s paternal grandmother Dolores Shelfo passed away on June 11, 2020, after gracing us with 85 years of life. She was an intelligent and academic youth, as well as a premiere athlete; a basketball player, bowler, and field hockey player to name a few. She graduated from Lindenhurst High School and attended Adelphi University on scholarship, where she was a Tri Delta, named May Queen, and achieved a physical education degree.

Dolores then met her husband Bill in Lindenhurst village, and after trying the knot on April 5, 1959, they went on to spend most of their lives on Long Island, New York before moving to Georgia in 2010.
