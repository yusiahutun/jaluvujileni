Young &amp; Restles Actors' Real-Life Partners

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, April 14, 2024
Credit: Jennifer Graylock/JPI, Jill Johnson/JPI, Gregg DeGuire/FilmMagic/Getty Images, David Crotty/Patrick MacMullan/Getty Images

  • Melissa Ordway and Justin Gaston

    The couple that acts together, stays together… at least that’s been the case for Abby’s portrayer and her handsome hubby, who tied the knot back in 2012. The couple — who have two daughters — got to tie the knot all over again when in late 2020, he temporarily stepped into the role of Chance just in time for their alter egos to become man and wife! 

  • Mishael Morgan and Navid Ali

    Since joining Young & Restless, Morgan’s played both the ill-fated Hilary Curtis Hamilton and the twin sister who never got to know her, Amanda Sinclair. In real life, the beauty has been married to her guy since since 2012. They have two children together.

  • Peter and Mariellen Bergman

    The handsome guy who plays Smilin’ Jack on Young and Restless married his second wife in 1985. At the time, he was playing All My Children good guy Cliff Warner. They have two children together.

  • Melody Thomas Scott and Edward J. Scott

    While mixing business and pleasure rarely works out for the residents of Genoa City, the same definitely can’t be said for Nikki’s portrayer and her hubby. Although now supervising producer at Bold & Beautiful, he was serving as executive producer at Young & Restless when first they met. 

  • Lauralee Bell and Scott Martin

    “Honestly, I couldn’t do what I do without his support,” Cricket’s longtime portrayer told us of the spouse to whom she’s been wed since 1997. The pair have two children who, as she puts it, “are probably old enough that they don’t need me hovering over them. But they still let me, anyways.” 

  • Joshua Morrow and Tobe Keeney

    After dating for five years, the couple tied the knot during a 2001 ceremony in Santa Barbara. “I love her to death,” he told Soaps In Depth, “and I truly don’t deserve her.” While we doubt that’s true, Nick’s portrayer added that “she has given me the four coolest children that I ever could have asked for!” 

  • Jason Thompson and Paloma Jonas

    Get ready, because Billy’s portrayer is about to gush about the beauty who became his bride in 2015 and has since gifted him with two children. “She’s just an amazing person and doesn’t have a selfish bone in her body,” Thompson told Soap Opera Digest. 

  • Melissa Claire Egan and Matt Katrosar

    While Egan and her spouse — whom she wed back in 2014 — have had the chance to work together on Young & Restless, Katrosar is not actually an actor. He did, however, put in two appearances on the soap on which she plays Chelsea in 2020, playing first her kidnapper and later an EMT! They welcomed their first child, baby boy Caden, into the world in August 2021 and their second, Jake, in July 2023.

  • Greg Rikaart and Rob Sudduth

    It may seem like just yesterday that Kevin’s portrayer exchanged vows with Sudduth, but they actually celebrated their sixth anniverary in 2021. “I wouldn’t have stayed sane this past year without you,” Rikaart told his hubby earlier this year on Instagram. “Well, I’d have gone more insane, I should say.” Together, they have one son, Montgomery, whom they affectionately call Monty. 

  • Eric Braeden and Dale Russell

    While Victor’s marriages have been legendarily rocky, Braeden’s own union has lasted more than five decades. And if there’s one thing anyone following him on social media knows, it’s how much he dotes on son Christian Gudegast’s adorable children! 

  • Eileen Davidson and Vince Van Patten

    Fans got an up-close and personal look into the homelife of Ashley’s on-again, off-again portrayer and her spouse when Davidson appeared on The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills. Married since 2003, they share one son. 

  • Doug Davidson and Cindy Fisher

    Davidson may no longer be onscreen as Paul — much to the consternation of many fans — but he did walk away with one heck of a parting gift: It was on the set of Young & Restless that he met his wife when she was playing Rebecca. Married in 1984, they have two children. 

  • Camryn Grimes and Brock Powell

    Mariah’s portrayer — who formerly played the ill-fated Cassie — and her voice-actor beau, Brock Powell, marked their first anniversary back in March of 2021. In January 2022, he popped the question and she said, “Yes!” The happy duo showed off the ring when they attended the Daytime Emmys together, and welcomed a baby boy named Bridger in December 2023.

  • Camryn Grimes and Brock Powell

    Young & Restless villain Robert Newman (ex-Ashland) has been married to Britt Helfer, who played Lily on Loving, since 1986. The pair has two kids together — Kendall and Connor.

  • Allison Lanier and JD-Samson

    According to her Instagram, Young & Restless’ newest Summer, Allison Lanier, shares a “birthday and a life” with non-binary singer/songwriter JD Samson.

  • Rory Gibson and Alicia Ruelas

    Rory Gibson, who plays Young & Restless’ Noah, has been in a relationship with model Alicia Ruelas for over a decade. They got engaged in October 2022!

  • Susan Walters and Linden Ashby

    Young & Restless’ Diane Jenkins, Susan Walters, is married to alum Linden Ashby, who just wrapped a return as big bad Cameron Kirsten. The pair tied the knot in 1986 and have two children, Grace and Savannah.

  • James Hyde and Sue-Ling Garcia Hyde

    Passions alum James Hyde, who played Young & Restless’ Jeremy Stark, married Sue-Ling Garcia in 1994. The good-looking duo has a son, James Moses.

  • Michael Damian and Janeen Damian

    Young & Restless’ favorite rock star, Michael Damian (Danny Romalotti) has been married to his lovely wife Janeen since 1998. They love to make beautiful movies together!

  • Shemar Moore and Jesiree Dizon

    Moore (ex-Malcolm) hid his lady love from the world until it was time for them to share the news that they were expecting their first child together. In January 2023, Moore revealed that Jesiree Dizon was his significant other and that she was pregnant with a girl. On January 24th, news broke that their daughter had been born. By all accounts, Moore dotes on his baby girl, Frankie.

  • Christel Khalil and Sam Restagno

    Young & Restless’s beleaguered Lily Winters hasn’t been so lucky in love, but Christel Khalil became engaged to her man, Sam Restagno, in April 2022, and they welcomed their first child together, Remy, in early 2024. The babe joined Khalil’s 13-year-old son, Michael Caden, from her first marriage to Stephen Hensley.

  • Michael Graziadei and Lauren Carey

    Michael Graziadei (Daniel Romalotti) surprised his longtime love Lauren Carey by popping the question on The Talk in March 2023. The pair has two twin boys, Oliver and Arlo, together.
