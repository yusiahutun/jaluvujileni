Best Firecracker Evolution Deck Clash Royale

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, August 31, 2024

The Firecracker Evolution is underway in Clash Royale. As part of this Rage and Fire challenge, you will need to make use of the Evolved version of the Firecracker Card. This unit now has explosive sparks with AoE and damage over time (similar to poison). It also has more HP and greater recoil. Because of this, you are going to need to build decks that revolve around maximizing the Evolved Firecracker’s new offensive potential. There are unlimited losses, so you don’t need to worry about getting this right in your very first try. But to help you do exactly that, we are here to present the best Firecracker Evolution Deck in Clash Royale.
