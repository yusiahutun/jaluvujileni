Karl Urban’s Net Worth

Karl Urban, the talented Kiwi actor from New Zealand, has amassed a net worth of $20 million. Born in Wellington in 1972, Urban’s early exposure to classic New Zealand cinema through his mother ignited his passion for the film industry.

He achieved his breakthrough in Hollywood with the 2002 film “Ghost Ship.” However, Urban is widely recognized for his notable roles, including Eomer in “The Lord of the Rings” trilogy, Dr. Leonard “Bones” McCoy in the “Star Trek” films, Vaako in “The Chronicles of Riddick” series, Kirill in “The Bourne Supremacy,” Judge Dredd in “Dredd,” and CIA agent William Cooper in “RED.”

Additionally, he gained acclaim in New Zealand for his performances in local films such as “The Price of Milk” and “Out of the Blue.” Urban also starred in the television show “Almost Human” from 2013 to 2014 as Detective John Kennex.

In September 2004, he married Natalie Wihongi, and the couple has two sons named Hunter and Indy. Currently, Karl and his family reside in Auckland, New Zealand.
