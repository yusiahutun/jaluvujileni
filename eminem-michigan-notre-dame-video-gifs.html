Eminem Goes 'Berzerk' at Michigan-Notre Dame Game [VIDEO, GIFs]

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, October 8, 2024

Eminem trolled a lot of college football fans last night (Sept. 7) at the Michigan-Notre Dame game. He was on hand to talk about 'Berzerk' and his new album, dropping in November ... and also to be super awkward, probably on purpose, because, well, he's Eminem.

Staring blankly and looking damn near catatonic, Em snapped out of his faux trance, explaining, "Sorry, live TV freaks me out a little bit. I'm sorry, what was the question?"

"It's usually like a two-day process, sometimes it can go to three. It's a lot of takes, a lot of sitting around waiting. But it's cool -- if the video comes out, I guess it's worth it."

When asked what he was most excited about for his new album, Em replied, "Nothing." He paused for a beat, then explained, "Nah, November 5, I'll probably be most excited to be done with it. It's gonna be called 'The Marshall Mathers LP II,' and it's gonna be a revisit to the first 'Marshall Mathers LP,' and that's probably all I can really say about it."

There's more, but you're probably better off seeing the moments that launched a thousand GIFs for yourself.
