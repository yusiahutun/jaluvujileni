Meet Hosea Williams III, Porsha Brother! His Bio, Age, Wife, Net Worth

Meet Hosea Williams III, brother of Porsha Williams from the show ‘Family Matters’. He is the oldest of the bunch and here are a few things you need to know about him.

Scroll down this Bio that covers his age, wife, net worth, and kids.

Meet Hosea Williams III, Porsha Brother

Hosea Williams III is the oldest of four children born to his late father Hosea Williams II. Hosea II had his son with his first wife Diane T Williams. He had two children with him, Hosea III and his sister Porsha Williams. After his parents divorced when he was around 5 years old and his sister was 3.

Then. Hosea III met their stepmother Andria (Lisa) Williams. He started a family with her and had another two children Brenton Williams and Lauren Williams, the youngest of the bunch. Although they came from separate family Hosea, Porsha, Brenton, and Lauren grew up very close to each other. They weren’t allowed to address each other as half-siblings.

But, when their father died on 21 September 1998 because of complications from a bone marrow transplant performed 1 1/2 months ago, the kids were left with each other and their two mothers.

Does Hosea Williams III Appear On Family Matters?

No, Hosea Williams III hasn’t appeared on ‘Family Matters’ yet.

The casts who appeared on the shows are his two sisters Porsha and Lauren, his mother Diane, Porsha’s husband Simon, Dennis McKinley, Gina Clayton, Drew Sidora, Shamea Morton, Yandy Smith-Harris, and Jennifer Williams.

Hosea Williams III Age

As written on his Facebook, Hosea Williams III was born in 1979. He turned 42 years old on 24 October. He often receives birthday wishes from his wife and his two reality star sisters Porsha and Lauren.

Hosea Williams III is married to his wife Rhonda Williams. They have been together at least since August 2014. Dating for more than three years, they engaged on 1 April 2017. Moreover, Rhonda seems to have two teenage kids from her previous relationships. Her daughter Kai turned 15 years old in June 2021. But, Rhonda isn’t the only one with kids. Hosea also has a daughter named Jadynn from her previous relationship.
