DMZ Disarming Presence, Drop ISO Hemlock Weapons in Dead Drop

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Sunday, February 18, 2024

In our DMZ Disarming Presence, Drop ISO Hemlock Weapons in Dead Drop guide, we are going to explain how to complete one of the steps in this mission. We will also give you some tips on how you can work around a bug where the Disarming Presence quest keeps resetting itself. Without further ado, let’s jump straight into it.

Drop 3 ISO Hemlock Weapons in Dead Drop DMZ Disarming Presence

To complete the “Drop 3 ISO Hemlock weapons in a dead drop” step in the DMZ Disarming Presence mission, you need to place three of those rifles into any Dead Drop in Vondel. The easiest way to do this is to go to the zoo in the south of the map and just farm AI enemies there. Eventually, they’ll drop three ISO Hemlocks. Pick them up and dump them into the Dead Drop, one of which is at the zoo. Just in case, drop the guns in one by one, not all three at once. I say this because the mission step sometimes refuses to complete if you place the ISO Hemlocks together. Speaking of bugs you might encounter…

DMZ Disarming Presence Keeps Resetting Bug Fix

The only potential fix for the bug where the DMZ Disarming Presence mission keeps resetting is to do all three steps in one deployment. That has apparently worked for some people. Also, it seems like you have to do everything on Vondel. Plus, you want to exfil with a basic version of the ISO Hemlock, such as the ones the AI enemies drop. Again, the zoo in Vondel is a good place to farm them. Mind you, executing everything perfectly does not guarantee that the mission will complete properly. It might just be that it’s bugged and will remain so until the developers intervene. In the meantime, all you can really do is submit a ticket to support and then just keep trying the mission over and over again.
