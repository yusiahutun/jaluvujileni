Jon Hamm’s Personal Life

Hamm was born in St Louis, Missouri, USA. His parents Deborah and Daniel got divorced when Hamm was two years old. After their divorce, Hamm grew up with his mother. After making a name in the acting industry, Hamm started dating actress and screenwriter Jennifer Westfeldt in 1997. The two had an 18-year-long relationship until they broke up in 2015. He was always very opposed to the idea of marriage, hence the couple never tied the knot.

During the final season of Mad Men, Hamm met Anna Osceola in 2015. The two began dating soon after and were in a happy relationship. On June 25, 2023, Hamm and Osceola tied the knot in a private ceremony held at Anderson Canyon, Big Sur, California. According to TMZ, the couple had a Mad Men-inspired wedding. They were joined by their friends from the industry such as Paul Rudd, Billy Crudup, and Tina Fey, among others.
