Meet Too Hot To Handle's Stevan Ditter! His Bio, Height, Parents, Age, Job

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, March 12, 2024

Meet Stevan Ditter, a fashion model based in LA, who has arrived at Netflix’s “Too Hot To Handle” villa. This article covers his height, his age, his birthday, and information on his parents as well as his siblings.

So, scroll down to learn more about him.

Stevan Ditter On Too Hot To Handle

Professional model Stevan Ditter is joining the casts of Netflix’s reality show ‘Too Hot To Handle.” The show is back for season 3 with ten hot singletons who are ready to have good times on-camera. The casts are going to spend four weeks on a tropical island in Turks and Caicos in a villa.

Similar to the first two seasons, the rules for the show remain the same. If there is any sexual act or even “kissing” involved under the supervision of Lana, the prize money deducts. However, the one thing that has been changed this time, is the prize money which has been doubled. The winner pockets $200 thousand. Since the stake is high casts this season has to rethink before they act which made it much more interesting for fans of the show to watch the casts struggle with their urges.

Stevan is a bad boy and was titled the “biggest flirt” back in high school. A self-described flirtatious, loud, and obnoxious person, he always manages to get attention wherever he arrives. Girls are all over him when he goes out to party.

Joining Stevan are her fellow casts Beaux Raymond, Georgia Hassarati, Harry Johnson, Holly Scarfone, Jaz Holloway, Nathan Soan Mingomezulu, Patrick Mullen, Patrick Mullen, Izzy Fairthorne, and Truth.

Before the premiere of the show, Stevan had announced, “ready for some chaos? catch me on @netflix on Jan 19″.

Stevan Ditter Girlfriend Type

Stevan Ditter is open to finding women he could have connections with. However, he is keeping the hopes of becoming an international playboy out of the villa door because, Lana, the virtual assistant, will certainly have her eyes on Stevan’s playboy acts.

How Old Is Stevan Ditter?

Stevan Ditter was born on 1 June 1995. He turned 26 years old in June 2021.

Stevan Ditter Parents

Stevan Ditte was born to his parents Michael Ditter and Lisa Ditter. According to his father’s Facebook, his parents tied the knots on 17 September 1992. So you know, they have been happily married for over 29 years.

More about Stevan’s father, Michael attended Longfellow Elementary School, Harpers Choice Middle School, and graduated from Centennial High School in 1982.

Moreover, Michael’s LinkedIn reveals that is the owner of Java Joe’s Charm City Bistro in Elkridge, Maryland. During the time of COVID-19 in 2020, his family paired with The University of Maryland Medical Center and Fairway Independent Mortgage, Ellicott City, and prepared and delivered lunches to almost 50 doctors and nurses on the pulmonology care team.

Besides running his coffee business with his wife, Michael also coaches soccer at the Soccer Association of Columbia (SAC). He is the head coach of 2009 boys and in 2019 the team won Amanda Post Tournament.

Moving on, Stevan’s mother Lisa Ditter is the owner of Salvage Art which she founded in 2015. According to Dun & Bradstreet, her company is part of the Miscellaneous Durable Goods Merchant Wholesalers Industry that is run with 3 total employees. Moreover, it generated $78,488 in sales in a year.

Lisa has also started a shop in Etsy with a theme (reclaim.revive.re-imagine). She has also created a Facebook page (@salvageart) and Instagram (@salvageartreclaim) page for her business. The business IG bio reads, “Rescuing + reviving vintage furniture + decorative elements for today’s contemporary design space.”

Most of the products she trades are furniture.

Coming back to her parents, Michael and Lisa, both of them are on Facebook (@michael.ditter and @lisa.ditter.1).

Stevan has also shared on his bio that his father Michael wanted to name him Evan whereas his mother named him Steve. Together they merged the names and became Stevan which he finds very interesting.

Besides his parents, Stevan has two younger siblings in his family. He has a brother named Brighton Ditter who has a down syndrome condition. Three years younger than Stevan, he turned 23 years old on 15 February 2021. His Facebook is (@brighton.ditter).

Stevan also has a sister named Paige Ditter (@paige.ditter). She is a student-athlete who played soccer at Long Reach High School. You can check out her Hudl and Maxpreps profile for more details regarding her soccer career. Furthermore, she was accepted at Penn State University where she will major in Telecommunications and Media Industries.

Stevan Ditter Height

According to his modeling profile on Verge Agency, Stevan stands tall at 6 feet 0.5 inches. He is a tattoo fanatic and has countless tattoos all over his body. But among all of them that certainly stands out are the date of birth of his parents that he inked on his biceps.

What Does Stevan Ditter Do For A Living?

As already stated, Stevan Ditter is a professional model. He has been signed to Wilhelmina Models, Next Model, and The Squad Management. He is currently represented by Verge Agency.

His Wilhelmina profile reads, “I model for mostly streetwear and editorial looks but am open to almost anything. My goal is to have modeling be my primary job. I want to use this site to make connections with people in the fashion world I wouldn’t have otherwise.”

Stevan clarified that he does only paid assignments and isn’t open to any nude modeling. He specializes in fit modeling, lifestyle, promotional, runway, swimwear, and sports modeling. He has posed for several notable photographers such as Kenneth Medilo for Vanity Teen.

Moreover, Stevan has modeled for clothing brands such as American Eagle Outfitters and Uni/Form LA.

In addition to modeling, Stevan is also pursuing a career in music. He goes by the name (@yattydaddy) on SoundCloud which only shelters one EDM song this is just the beginning. His bio reads, “jockey of discs”. And, he also runs his own clothing brand Novelty Noize which makes necklaces and chokers.

But, before debuting his modeling right after high school, Stevan was also a student-athlete who played soccer. He played ‘Forward’ as a primary position and ‘Attacking Midfielder’ as a secondary position. In his NCA Sports Org profile, he shared that he started playing soccer at the age of 3.

Stevan added, “I have been the highest and strongest player on my team constantly scoring and assisting goals as well as captaining teams at games and in practices. In the off-season, I set up indoor leagues with my high school team and play travel indoor, travel futsal, and travel outdoor in the spring. I play hard from beginning to end, game or practice.”

Talking about his education, Stevan graduated high school from Long Reach High School as a class of 2013.
