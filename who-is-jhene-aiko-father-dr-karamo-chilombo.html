Who is Jhen Aiko father Dr. Karamo Chilombo?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Sunday, February 4, 2024

Dr. Chill, also known as Dr. Karamo Chilombo, is a trained American physician and vocalist. He is well-known for being the father of the musicians Jhene Aiko and Mila J.

One of the key reasons why many of his admirers want to learn more about him is because he loves what he does.

Who is Jhené Aiko’s father Dr. Karamo Chilombo?

The father of Jhene Aiko and a medical specialist, Dr. Karamo Chilombo, just welcomed his ninth child.

The 78-year-old announced via a social media post that included images of the newborn.

Chilombo not only practiced medicine but also had a prosperous singing career. He was born Gregory Wycliff Barnes on December 1, 1944, and as of 2022, he will be 76 years old.

The father of the Bakersfield, California native is both German-Jewish and African-American. His mother is of Dominican, Dominican, Japanese, and Spanish ancestry.

He received his medical degree from the University of California, San Francisco, in 1974, and in 1978 he started working at the Case Western Reserve University School of Medicine in Cleveland, Ohio.

He became a licensed medical specialist in 1978 after receiving his credentials and licensure.

At the moment, Dr. Karamo Chilombo works for Los Angeles Community Hospital which is connected to Monterey Park Hospital. He has extensive experience as a pediatrician.
