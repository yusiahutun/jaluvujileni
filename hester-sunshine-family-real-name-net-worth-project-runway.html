Now, as a competitor on Project Runway All-Star Season 20, they are going to be smitten by style and aesthetics even more. So, in this writing, let us tell you more about them including her family background and net worth.

Of course, they were also anyway never turning down the chance to test herself against other top-tier artists.

Hester’s designs are infused with the otherness present in their own lives and in both their queer and Jewish identities.

They were six the first time they told people they are going to be a fashion designer.

Hester Sunshine Family

Hester’s father Philip Sunshine is no more. He died on 24 August 2021 at age 81, after many health struggles. He passed of heart failure, at home in bed listening to his favorite composer, Sibelius. The patriarch was a high school history teacher for over 30 years.

Late Philip is also survived by his wife of 52 years, Jody (Judith Morris), and many pet dogs and cats.

Hester’s mother, Jody, reached age 83 in April 2023. Formerly, Jody was an art teacher at Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School. Even before, she studied at New York University, Boston University, Parsons School of Design, and Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts. As of now, she had been calling Santa Fe, New Mexico home.
