Annual 5K Run/Walk Honors Bloomingdale Firefighter

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, August 5, 2024

The race will be in memory of longtime borough resident Bob Kochka, at 11 a.m., on Saturday, Sept. 26, at 225 Glenwild Ave.

Kochka served for nearly 23 years as lieutenant and captain of the Bloomingdale Fire Department before he lost his battle to pancreatic cancer in 2013.

Proceeds from the race will go toward the Lustgarten Foundation for Pancreatic Cancer Research.
