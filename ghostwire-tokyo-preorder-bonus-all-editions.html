Ghostwire Tokyo Preorder Bonus &amp; All Editions

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Thursday, March 21, 2024

If you are not sure which Ghostwire Tokyo edition is the right pick for you, we’ve got you covered. The next action-horror experience from Tango Gameworks is just around the corner, and now is the right time to decide if you want to preorder the game or wait for the release. If you want to battle the supernatural forces which are invading Tokyo with style, there are some fancy garments that you can get. Read on to learn about the Ghostwire Tokyo pre-order bonus and all the different Ghostwire Tokyo editions.

Ghostwire Tokyo Preorder Bonus Content

Ghostwire: Tokyo is set to be released on March 25th, 2022. At the time of writing, you have around a week to decide if you wish to pre-order the game. But, what Ghostwire Tokyo bonus content will you receive if you pre-order the game? That depends on the platform you pick. If you are a PC gamer, you’ll obtain the Hannya outfit as a pre-order bonus. But, there’s so much more if you are gaming on the PlayStation 5. Those who pre-order Ghostwire for the PS5 will get the Biker outfit premium pack, in addition to the Hannya outfit. The Biker outfit premium pack comes with nine colour variations! And that’s not all, because there’s more! On top of that, PS Plus members are eligible for a 10% discount if they pre-order Ghostwire Tokyo.

Ghostwire Tokyo Deluxe and Standard Edition Difference

Now, when you know what comes as a Ghostwire Tokyo preorder bonus, let’s learn about different editions. Unlike many modern games, here we have just two basic editions. The good old Deluxe and Standard editions. The standard edition is, well, just a basic game and pre-order bonuses. On the other hand, the Deluxe edition packs a whole lot more goodies. Get the Ghostwire Tokyo Deluxe Edition and receive the following bonus content:

  • Shinobi Outfit
  • Kunai Weapon
  • Streetwear Fashion Pack (four Outfits)

On top of that, there’s a special bonus for the owners of the PS5 Ghostwire Tokyo Deluxe Edition. Pre-order it now and get an exclusive 3-day early access. With that said, that’s all you need to know about the Ghostwire: Tokyo Preorder Bonus and the Deluxe Edition content.
