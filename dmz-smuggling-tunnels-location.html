DMZ Smuggling Tunnels Location

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, May 14, 2024

Figuring out where to find the DMZ Smuggling Tunnels location in Warzone 2 is important for one of the Tier 2 White Lotus missions. The real problem here is that the entrance to these tunnels is in the middle of nowhere and therefore very easy to overlook. So, in this guide, we are going to help you locate the DMZ Smuggling Tunnels.

Locate the Smuggling Tunnels DMZ

To find the location of the DMZ Smuggling Tunnels in COD Warzone 2, you need to look for them in the middle between Rohan Oil and Al Mazrah City. It’s a little difficult to describe in words, because the location is really in the middle of nowhere. So, I’d recommend you take a look at the map below to see where you need to go. It counts as a stronghold at the moment, but who knows whether that will always be the case. What you’re looking for is an entrance into the tunnels. You’ll recognize it by the blown-open gates. Once you know where the entrance is, you shouldn’t have too much trouble spotting it, since it’s kinda the only thing of note in the vicinity.
