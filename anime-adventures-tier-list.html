Anime Adventures Tier List

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, January 14, 2024

Anime Adventures is an extremely popular Roblox game, and with good reason. This game combines Tower Defense-style gameplay with characters from some of the most beloved anime franchises of all time. This includes characters from Naruto, JoJo’s Bizarre Adventure, My hero Academia, Dragonball Z, Attack On Titan, and more. With a game such as this, it’s imperative that you know which characters are the strongest ones, so that you can make the best possible selection. We are here to rank all of them in our Anime Adventures Tier List.

Anime Adventures Tier List

Anime Adventures Best and Worst Characters Ranked Tier List

There are several dozen characters available to play in Anime Adventures. As the game is being worked on, more are being added to it all the time. So think of this guide as a work in progress, one that we’re going to revisit from time to time when new updates and characters arrive. Also, you need to be aware that we only take raw power rankings into account here. Things such as how easy it is to get a certain character, their look, backstory, and so on, don’t factor into it.
