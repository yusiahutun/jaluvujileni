Zelda TOTK Zora Armor Upgrade Cost and Materials

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, September 6, 2024

Not sure what are the Zora Armor set upgrade material requirements in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom? We’ve got you covered! The legendary Zora Armor is back, and it looks as good as ever. It is the best armor for anything involving water in TOTK, and it’s a great choice if you’re considering the upgrade. In this guide, we provide the Zora Armor upgrade cost in Zelda TOTK, alongside Zora Helm and Zora Greaves.

Zelda TOTK Zora Armor Upgrade Materials

Zora Armor Upgrade Cost and Materials in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom

If you intend to swim a lot in Zelda Tears of the Kingdom, the Zora Armor set is the right thing for you! Each set piece increases swim speed further. Of course, the best thing is that you can swim up waterfalls! Finally, if you wear the whole set – it reduces the swim dash stamina cost. However, as with all other sets, in order to activate the reduced swim dash stamina cost, you will need to upgrade Zora Armor set. If you are planning on doing such things, you are probably wondering which materials you need. Below you will find a complete list of materials for each Zora Armor upgrade level:

  • Zora Armor
    • 1st Level – 10 Rupees, Lizalfos Horn x3
    • 2nd Level – 50 Rupees, Lizalfos Talon x5 and Hyrule Bass
    • 3rd Level – 200 Rupees, Blue Lizalfos Horn x5, Lizalfos Tail x3, Hearty Bass x3
    • 4th Level – 500 Rupees, Black Lizalfos Horn x5, Blue Lizalfos Tail x5, and Opal x20
  • Zora Helm
    • 1st Level – 10 Rupees, Lizalfos Horn x3
    • 2nd Level – 50 Rupees, Lizalfos Talon x5 and Hyrule Bass
    • 3rd Level – 200 Rupees, Blue Lizalfos Horn x5, Lizalfos Tail x3, Hearty Bass x3
    • 4th Level – 500 Rupees, Black Lizalfos Horn x5, Blue Lizalfos Tail x5, and Opal x20
  • Zora Greaves
    • 1st Level – 10 Rupees, Lizalfos Horn x3
    • 2nd Level – 50 Rupees, Lizalfos Talon x5 and Hyrule Bass
    • 3rd Level – 200 Rupees, Blue Lizalfos Horn x5, Lizalfos Tail x3, Hearty Bass x3
    • 4th Level – 500 Rupees, Black Lizalfos Horn x5, Blue Lizalfos Tail x5, and Opal x20
