Is there going to be a second season?

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, January 15, 2024

The Enemy Within fans is left distressed as NBC has not announced the season’s reinstatement yet. Fans are patiently waiting to know about the future of police procedural.

[fvplayer id=”1279″]

Although the NBC network has already announced the fall 2019-2020 TV schedule, The Enemy Within fans are off the edge of their chairs.

The Enemy Within season 2 fate depends on the numbers

The network has recently aired the season finale of The Enemy Within the first installment. Before giving another treat to the fans, NBC is thoroughly observing the viewership numbers to justify the ordering of the second installment.

To answer what the numbers say, the average rating of The Enemy Within is of .75. The 18-49 demographic has steadily attracted 4.4 million viewers a week.

According to a TV series finale report, The Enemy Within secures number 10 for NBC’s scripted shows in the years 2018 and 2019.

The Enemy Within season 2 is followed by AP Bio and The Village in the category of series that are yet to be renewed by the network.

The Enemy Within Season 2 has high renewal chances

Going with what numbers say, The Enemy Within stands a high chance of renewal by the NBC. The show’s reach is expected to grow by a considerable margin.

The Enemy Within season 2 will take over from where Shepheard (Jennifer Carpenter) was offered a job within the counter-terrorism department by Keaton (Morris Chestnut). It is likely to open up many possibilities of the storyline.

The Enemy Within 2: Shepherd and Keaton

According to a report by TV Insider, The Enemy Within Season 2 will focus on flourishing the relationship between Shepherd and Keaton. The characters will have a bigger villain looming at them.

The relationship between Shepherd and Keaton did not start well. Shepherd was seen compromising previous agents and then shooting Keaton on her clavicle.

It would be exciting for the fans to see where this relationship goes. Fans would also love to see writers to set up the villain’s death in an interesting way in The Enemy Within season 2.
