Cassidy Black Biography, Children, Family, Facts About Dierks Bentleys Wife

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, April 16, 2024

A talented husband, a close family and a romance that lasts forever, it has everything that makes a great story. Cassidy Black may have burst into the limelight thanks to her successful husband, but she’s remained a mistress in her own right. She may be widely known as Dierks Bentley’s wife, but there’s a lot more than meets the eye on his sweetheart. Here’s a little insight into the life of the woman after Dierk’s heart.

Cassidy Black’s bio

Cassidy Black did a great job of keeping her private life out of the spotlight. It is obvious that this is not an easy task when the person in question belongs to a large star. Given this fact, it is not surprising that there is not so much information about her childhood and upbringing. Aside from the fact that she was born on January 8, 1975, there is no other information that describes her childhood.

Still, the way she ended up with her husband is a love story worth telling. She actually met the star when they were in middle school in Phoenix, Arizona. Sometimes people have the most unconventional ways of getting together and this is one of those cases. They didn’t run in the same circle until Dierk started dating her best friend. Soon after, Cassidy Black and her then-husband began dating. The pattern continued even after middle school, until 2005, when she visited him on the street in Las Vegas. Apparently she got on his bus and found a permanent place in his heart.

Ten months later he proposed and they immediately eloped to Mexico. Their love has only increased greatly since then, as mentioned earlier, their love story was for the ages.

family & children

One thing is evident about Cassidy Black: the fact that she is all about her family. In addition to her already known husband, she has three children. The couple is blessed with two boys and one girl and are named Knox, Evie and Jordan. Family life takes its toll on couples in the spotlight, but these two have learned how to navigate the maze of family, career, and spotlight. Before his marriage, her husband couldn’t understand how married men on tour couldn’t wait to get home to their families. But now he’s joined the bandwagon thanks to how awesome Cassidy and the kids were.

Speaking of the home, there’s something quite unconventional about the actual structure of the building. Cassidy Black calls it “a piece of cake” because it’s practically in the shape of a triangle. Her husband equally refers to it as a “pie in the sky with a youthful centerpiece”. All in all it’s a place where everyone can feel at home and the quirks just make it better.
