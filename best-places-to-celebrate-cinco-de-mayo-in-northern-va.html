Best Places To Celebrate Cinco De Mayo In Northern VA

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, September 24, 2024

Whether it's nachos, tacos or margaritas you're looking for, there are plenty of spots in Northern Virginia to try.

Here are some of the highest rated Mexican restaurants on Yelp and Google to try:

La Campesina Restaurant, 8442 Lee Highway, Fairfax

Taco Zocalo,  1810 Michael Faraday Dr., Reston

Poco Loco Mexican Restaurant, 5414 Mudd Tavern Road, Thornburg

Tacos El Chilango Food Truck, 1723 14th St N., Arlington

Tortas Y Tacos La Chiquita, 2911 Columbia Pike, Arlington

Los Tios Grill, 2615 Mount Vernon Avenue, Alexandria
