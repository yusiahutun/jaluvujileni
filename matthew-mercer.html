What is Matthew Mercer’s net worth? Who is Matthew Mercer’s wife?

Introduction

Matthew Mercer, simply known as Matt Mercer, is an anime, film, animation, and video game voice actor who provided his voice to notable anime series like Attack on Titan and Magi: The Labyrinth of Magic.

Also, in video games, he provided his voice for:

  • Edér Teylecg in Pillars of Eternity;
  • MacCready in Fallout 4;
  • Yusuke Kitagawa in Persona 5;
  • McCree in Overwatch;
  • Jack Cooper in Titanfall 2;
  • Chrom in Fire Emblem;
  • Leon S. Kennedy in Resident Evil.

In anime, he provided his voice for:

  • Trafalgar Law in the Funimation dub of One Piece;
  • Kanji Tatsumi for the second half of Persona 4: The Animation;
  • Jotaro Kujo in Jojo’s Bizarre Adventure;
  • Kiritsugu Emiya in Fate/Zero;
  • Levi Ackerman in Attack on Titan.

Matt is also the Dungeon Master for the main campaigns of Critical Role.

Biography

@Getty

He was born on June 29, 1982, as Matthew Christopher Miller, in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, USA.

One of his first roles as a voice actor was in the video game Star Trek: Shattered Universe (2003). He said in an interview:

”I first got into voice acting, my very, very first taste of it was in the early 2000s.”

Matt later added:

”It was enough to kind of give me the idea that there was something to this.”

Critical Role

Matthew Mercer, Marisha Ray, Taliesin Jaffe, Laura Bailey, Sam Riegel, Ashley Johnson, Liam O’Brien and Travis Willingham – @Getty

Since 2012, Matt has been the Dungeon Master for Critical Role, where he runs a group of voice actors through a campaign of Dungeons & Dragons streamed on Twitch every Thursday. The group consists of:

  • the Aasimar Barbarian – Yasha (Ashley Johnson)
  • the Human Wizard – Caleb Widogast (Liam O’Brien)
  • the Goblin Rogue – Nott the Brave (Sam Riegel)
  • the Tiefling Blood Hunter – Mollymauk Tealeaf and Firbolg Cleric—Caduceus Clay (Taliesin Jaffe);
  •  the Human Monk – Beauregard (Marisha Ray);
  • the Half-Orc Warlock – Fjord (Travis Willingham);
  • the Tiefling Cleric – Jester (Laura Bailey).

His work as Dungeon Master has led to a campaign book being published through Green Ronin Publishing called Critical Role: Tal’Dorei Campaign Setting. During an interview, Matthew said:

”I’d heard about D&D when I was a kid. Like I had seen pictures, and had friends who had books, but never had the opportunity to really be invited to it.”

In a different interview, Mercer declared:

”My decision to DM this game stems from my love of storytelling and watching others play in a sandbox of my own design.”

He acted in several shows from Geek & Sundry (a commercial YouTube channel and multimedia production company that was launched on April 2, 2012, by actress Felicia Day with Sheri Bryant and Kim Evey as part of YouTube’s 100 million dollars original channel initiative) and Nerdist.

Matt produced the web series Fear News with the Last Girl.

Matthew Ryan Key, Brittany Walloch, Greg De Stefano, Jennifer Newman, Marisha Ray, Matthew Mercer, Grant Imahara and Meredith Placko at the 2015 Renaissance Pleasure Faire – @Getty

In 2015, Mercer began voicing the character Bigfoot on the Cartoon Network series Wabbit.

He is also known for Overwatch (2016). 

In 2017, Matthew was the Dungeon Master for the Nerdist show CelebriD&D, which puts D&D-playing celebrities into a small, mini-campaign where they are paired with role-players.

He has given his voice to the well-known animated movie – Resident Evil: Damnation.

In 200, Matt performed in Blood of Zeus, Family Guy, Popup Dungeon, Amphibia, Robot Chicken, American Dad!, Close Enough, Chico Bon Bon: Monkey with a Tool Belt, and Ghost in the Shell SAC_2045.

Wife
