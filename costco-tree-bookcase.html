Costco Is Selling the Most Magical Bookcases

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, July 22, 2024

If you purchase an independently reviewed product or service through a link on our website, SheKnows may receive an affiliate commission.

Nothing brings a cozy feel to a home quite like a bookcase stacked with novels, picture books, and trinkets and vases on display. It’s a personal touch that turns what could be an annoying and messy stack of books and magazines into part of the room’s decor. But you don’t have to stick to your classic rectangular bookcases. If you’re looking for the perfect bookcase for kids, or if you want a unique bookcase for your own space, then look no further than Costco. The warehouse giant is currently selling the most whimsical tree-shaped bookcase, and while it’s intended to be a bookcase for kids, we can totally see adults with eclectic tastes rocking it in the living room, too.

The Delta Children Tree Shaped Bookcase at Costco is nearly four feet tall, and has lots of different storage areas for displaying knicknacks and toys. Instagrammer CostcoHotFinds, who spotted the tree shaped bookcase at Costco being sold for $119.99, also mentioned that she thinks her kid “would have loved playing with his action figures on it.” We can even see this tree bookcase being transformed into a playhouse for Calico Critters, or into a dollhouse. As for adults, it’s a colorful spot to display all sorts of collectibles and other unique items you want on display. It could be used to hold house plants, too.

Delta Children Tree Bookcase

Costco is currently sold out of the Delta Children Tree Bookcase online, so members (sign up for a membership here) will have to look for this one in stores. But what if you’re not a Costco member? Never fear.

The Delta Children Tree-Shaped Bookcase is available online on Amazon for $199.99, and from Sam’s Club, where it’s just $149.98. Sam’s Club is also selling a similarly enchanting Unicorn Shaped Bookcase from the same brand, and it currently holds a five-star rating.

Delta Children Unicorn Shaped Bookcase

We can totally see this brightening up a playroom, nursery, or bedroom. It also seems rife with DIY potential — a coat of chalkboard paint could be fun, and we’ve never said no to glitter spray paint.

Using one of these whimsically shaped bookcases is also a great way to make reading seem more fun to reluctant kids. It will seem a lot more appealing to choose a book from a tree-shaped bookcase than from a boring one. But the same goes for us grown-ups, too! A little whimsy in the home never hurts, no matter how old you are. Whether you use this tree-shaped bookcase for the kids, for your books, for your plants, or to display your collections (crystals would look cute!), it will definitely add some magic to your home.
