Meet Kandi &amp; The Gang's Melvin Jones! His Bio, Age, Height, Dating

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, May 15, 2024

Most of the Old Lady Gang employee featured on Kandi & the Gang has familial ties with the Burruss and Tucker family. And our star, Melvin Jones — the kitchen manager — made it into the family restaurant as Kandi’s nephew, aunt Bertha’s grandson, and Patrick Dallas’ cousin.

Follow this Melvin Jones Bio if you wish to learn more about him. 

Melvin Jones On Kandi & The Gang

Bravo’s new docuseries, Kandi & the Gang hit the small screen on March 6, 2022, and just as the title suggests, it saw Kandi Burruss and her “gang” including Melvin Jones trying their best to keep the restaurant running.

Yes! while you may think working hand to hand with a family member is much easier, this show has proven otherwise. It featured coworkers clashing, Burruss’ family members fighting with the waitstaff, and there were even a few hookups caught on camera.

Even Melvin was having a hard time after Todd had some concerns about the kitchen. Being dealt a difficult hand at the restaurant, Melvin was struggling to keep his job. A few even believed that he would have to say goodbye to the restaurant for the greater good of OLG.

Joining Melvinthis season were his co-stars — Patrick Dallas, Shawndreca Robinson, Dom’Unique Variety, Brian Redmond, Torin Mitchell, Brandon Black, Phillip Frempong, and Rashard Roles.

According to the show’s lead Kandi, Kandi & the Gang celebrates the intersectionality of its black and queer stars. Especially, she believes that it was the men in particular who made her restaurant shine.

And it’s true. They’ve got a lot of Black charming men on the show — some straight, some gay, and there “are masculine-gay and then some that are not as masculine,” Kandi explained.

All excited to see himself on a billboard in Times Square, Melvin took it to his IG on March 2, 2022, to write, “Grind will turn into your shine be patient‼️ mama I’m on a billboard in New York.”

Also, 2 days later, he shed a little light about his time on the show through an IG post captioned, “Stereotype of a black male misunderstood, but it’s still all good.”

How Much Is Melvin Jones Net Worth?

Melvin Jones garnered a net worth of above $100 thousand by 2022.

A chef by profession, Melvin was working as the kitchen manager at the Old Lady Gang then. Reportedly, an executive chef in Atlanta, GA made around $77 thousand per annum. So, since Melvin was working in a high-end restaurant, he must have been paid handsomely.

When Melvin isn’t in the kitchen, he likes to spend time with his family and friends.

As for his education, Melvin graduated from Heritage H.S and later went on to join Presbyterian College for his bachelor’s in business administration.

During his time at Presbyterian, Melvin was in the college’s football team and played nine games in 2009 as Defensive Line. Also, back when he was playing for Chad Frazier at Heritage H.S, he was named “Defensive Player of the year for Rockdale County” and went on to be named to the ESPN 150 defensive line list at No. 64.

Circa 2021, he was also involved with the Art Institute of Atlanta.

Melvin Jones Age

Melvin Jones was born on  May 27, 1991. That made him 30 years of age when he appeared on Kandi & the Gang in 2022.

As per his birthday, Melvin is of the Gemini zodiac.

Melvin Jones Height

Melvin Jones stands tall at a height of 6 feet (183 cm) and weighs 140 kg (309 lbs).

Mentioning his distinct features — Melvin has tattoos over his right hand, and he rocked that bald look with a thick beard.

Is Melvin Jones Dating Anyone?

Melvin Jones didn’t seem to be dating anyone as of 2022.

The chef was busy building his career and making a good name for himself then. So, perhaps Melvin never had time for a girlfriend.

If you didn’t know, Melvin has spent some time in jail back in 2012. Reportedly, he was arrested for allegedly stealing a micro-refrigerator and a television.

Did you know: Melvin’s full name is “Willie Melvin Jones.”

Related FAQs

  • Where Is Melvin Jones From?

Melvin hails from McDonough, GA.

  • What Do We Know About Melvin Jones Family?

Melvin comes from Kandi’s side of the family. He is the son of Melvin Jones and the grandson of Bertha Jones.

His cousin Patrick and grandma Bertha are also his co-stars from Kandi & the Gang.

A childhood pic of Melvin and Patrick showed that the two have always loved each other unconditionally. Also, Melvin was super close with his grandmother.

On Bertha’s 80th birthday (i.e. on November 17, 2021), Melvin took it to his IG to write, “80 never looked so good, my fav mean lady, but has a real heart of gold, but ain’t going for nothing.”

  • Is Melvin Jones On Instagram?

Yes, as of March 2022, find Melvin on Instagram @chef_melvinj with 9.5K followers.

Most of his posts then featured his time on Kandi & the Gang, his family and friends, and foods.
