Destiny 2 First Crota Team's Fallen Dead Ghost Location in World's Grave

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, February 6, 2024

The Dead Ghost in First Crota Team’s Fallen Destiny 2 Shadowkeep is an item that you have to find in World’s Grave to complete the Dead Ghost Trace quest. As is the case with other Destiny 2 Shadowkeep Dead Ghost Traces, the First Crota Team’s Fallen dead ghost is really well-hidden, in a side room in The Worlds Grave that you can easily miss. With that in mind, welcome to our Destiny 2 First Crota Team’s Fallen Dead Ghost Location in World’s Grave guide to show you exactly where to find it.

Where to Find Dead Ghost in First Crota Team’s Fallen Destiny 2 Shadowkeep Quest?

To find the First Crota Team’s Fallen Dead Ghost in Destiny 2 Shadowkeep World’s Grave, we’re gonna start from the entrance into Temple of Crota. Go inside the temple, drop down to the lower platform, and continue straight through the doorway in front of you. Drop down another level, and turn around. Follow the corridor past the glowing globe, and just keep going down that path. At the end of the winding stairs, turn right, and you’ll find a large, circular room with a giant, pointy… thing in the middle. In this room, you can find the Essence of Greed Ethereal Charms.

Go inside the above-described room, then head to the right and through the doorway. Follow this new path, and you’ll soon be in The World’s Grave. Keep going through the cavern and head left. Press on until you enter another big, circular room with a well-like structure in the middle. Go through the doorway on the right, and climb up the stairs. Turn left, and go into the side room. Look into the corner on the left, and you’ll find the Dead Ghost among the damaged tanks.
