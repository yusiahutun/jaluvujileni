SW Jedi Survivor Stuck on Loading Screen Fix

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, April 25, 2024

If you need a fix for SW Jedi Survivor getting stuck on the loading screen, you’re in the right place. We are going to walk you through several possible ways you can solve this problem. It’s incredibly annoying to stare at the same screen for what seems to be infinity without knowing whether it will ever go beyond. So, here’s what you can do about it.

How to Fix Star Wars Jedi Survivor Stuck on Loading Screen Issue

There are several potential solutions for the issue of SW Jedi Survivor getting stuck on the loading screen. First off, there’s the all the usual stuff you need to check. First off, does your rig meet at least the minimum requirements to run the game? If not, well, there’s your problem. Next, make sure to update all of your GPU drivers and install the latest DirectX packages, then restart your computer and try again. Other things you can try is to disable your antivirus / firewall and see whether that helps, as well as running the game as the administrator. These are all common things, I know, but they all bear repeating. If none of them work, here’s what to do next.

Probably the most useful fix for SW Jedi Survivor getting stuck on the loading screen is to verify and repair game files. On Steam, you do that, right-click the game in your library and select Properties. Click the Local Files tab on the left, then Verify Integrity of Game Files, and wait for the process to finish before trying again. On the Epic Games Store, click the three dots next to Jedi Survivor in your library, then Manage, the Verify. On the EA app, click Star Wars Jedi Survivor in the library, then Manage, then Repair. This should take care of the loading issue for most people, provided that everything else is up to code. If you have any other suggestions, share them in the comments.
