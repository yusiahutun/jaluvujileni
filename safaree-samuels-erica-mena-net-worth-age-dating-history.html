Safaree Samuels Is Set To Become A Father With Wife Erica Mena; His Dating History Revealed

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Sunday, September 1, 2024

It seems like Jamaican rapper Safaree Samuels has it all. Apart from a successful career, he also enjoys a prosperous marriage with his TV personality wife, Erica Mena.

Further, he is also set to become a father soon. With all his dreams coming true, it looks like Safaree Samuels will soon be running out of things to wish for.

Engaged After Dating A Month, Wedding In New York

Safaree started dating Erica Mena in November 2018. Only after a month of dating, the couple got engaged on 24 December 2018.

Less than a year post their engagement, Safaree and his blushing fiancee decided to upgrade their relationship even further.

The pair got married on 7 October 2019 at a wedding ceremony, which took place in New York. The details of the wedding were kept a mystery as both Safaree Samuels and Erica Mena were starring in the 10th season of Love & Hip Hop New York.

The ceremony was later featured in an episode of the reality series as fans envied Erica Mena's dashing white gown. Meanwhile, Safaree was dressed in a red-colored suit.

Awaiting First Child Together

By the time the wedding took place, Erica Mena was already pregnant with the couple's first child together. The duo first shared the news of the pregnancy on 1st October 2019. Later, Erica shared a sonogram of her upcoming baby to jollify her fans.

Safaree and his wife are now patiently waiting for the arrival of their child.

Safaree Samuels with pregnant wife Erica Mean in January 2020 (©: Instagram/Safaree Samuels)

Though the upcoming baby is the first for Safaree, he/she will be the second for Erica. She has a son named King Javien Conde- who was born on 1 March 2007. King Javien was born from Erica's previous relationship with Raul Conde.

Now, with another baby coming soon, Safaree and Erica will be parenting two kids together.

Safaree Samuels' Dating History Consists of 11 Women

Safaree's dating history is quite long. He dated 11 women before finally dating down with Erica Mena. Immediately, prior to starting a relationship with Erica, Safaree was dating Gabrielle Davis. 

The former couple dated for a while in 2016 but soon broke up. But, despite the split, the two kept in touch and even rekindled their romance for a while in 2018. However, the hopes for a new start died down after Safaree commenced his relationship with Erica.

Other than Gabrielle, Safaree has ten more ex-girlfriends, including Nicki Minaj, Candice Brooks, Lyrica Anderson, Mariahlynn, DreamDoll, Star Divine, Nikki Mudarris, Analicia Chaves, Zashia Santiago, and Sophia Body.

Bio Reveals Birthday, Height & Net Worth 

A native of New York, Safaree Samuels was born on 4 July 1981 (age: 38). He boasts a height of 6 feet 1 inch (1.85 m). 

A multi-talented man, he is known as a TV personality, actor, rapper, and songwriter. However, he is most famous as a music producer. Throughout his career, Safaree has worked with several artists such as Nicki Minaj, Philly Swain, Bounty Killer, Ward 21, 7even Up, and more.

For his efforts in the field, he has been rewarded well. Safaree has already secured a net worth of $3 million, but the amount is set to rise way up in the future.

Inked Tattoos For Ex-Girlfriend, Removes Soon After Break Up

An art enthusiast, Safaree, has inked multiple tattoos all over his body, including a Michael Jackson tattoo on his right hand.

Once he started dating Nicki Minaj, the rapper also got a tattoo resembling her face on his right chest while imprinting her name on the left side.

Safaree Samuels had tattooed Nicki Minaj's face and name while the two were dating (©: Daily Mail)

However, once they broke up, he soon covered the tattoos smartly. Now, it looks like Nicki's tattoo on his chest never existed.
