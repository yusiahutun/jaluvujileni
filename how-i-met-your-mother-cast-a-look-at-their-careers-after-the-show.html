A Look at Their Careers After the Show

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Friday, May 3, 2024

Nearly a decade after the finale of How I Met Your Mother, the show’s legacy continues to echo in the halls of TV history. The CBS sitcom, which aired from 2005 to 2014, not only captured the hearts of millions with its unique storytelling and memorable catchphrases but also launched its cast members into varying trajectories of success. As we catch up with each of the main cast members, it’s clear their careers have taken as many twists and turns as the plot of their long-running series.

Josh Radnor as Ted Mosby


Josh Radnor, forever etched in our memories as the love-seeking architect Ted Mosby from How I Met Your Mother, has successfully branched out into various artistic avenues since the show’s conclusion. Radnor’s post-sitcom career includes significant roles in the TV dramas Mercy Street and Hunters, and he showcased his versatility in the FX miniseries Fleishman Is in Trouble. His passion for music culminated in the release of several studio albums, where he explores a range of themes and sounds, further proving his multifaceted talent.

In his personal life, Radnor recently celebrated a new beginning, marrying clinical psychologist Jordana Jacobs in a picturesque, snow-covered ceremony in January 2024. This new chapter not only marks a personal milestone for Radnor but also highlights his journey from a beloved TV character to a well-rounded artist and family man.

Cobie Smulders as Robin Scherbatsky


Cobie Smulders, renowned for her role as the tough-as-nails Robin Scherbatsky in How I Met Your Mother, seamlessly transitioned into the action-packed world of the Marvel Cinematic Universe as the formidable Maria Hill. Beyond her superhero persona, Smulders has showcased her acting versatility in the Netflix series Friends from College and portrayed a compelling role in Impeachment: American Crime Story.

Off-screen, Smulders’s life is equally vibrant and fulfilling. Married to comedian Taran Killam, the couple balances their Hollywood careers with raising two daughters. Her journey from a beloved sitcom star to a key player in one of the biggest film franchises exemplifies her dynamic career progression. Smulders continues to captivate audiences, proving her ability to diversify her roles while maintaining a stable and enriching personal life.

Neil Patrick Harris as Barney Stinson


Neil Patrick Harris, famed for his charismatic portrayal of Barney Stinson on How I Met Your Mother, has continued to captivate and charm audiences with his diverse range of talents. On Broadway, Harris delivered a stunning performance in Hedwig and the Angry Inch, earning critical acclaim for his dynamic presence and vocal prowess. His versatility extended to the screen with a lead role in Netflix’s A Series of Unfortunate Events, where he brought the whimsical and nefarious Count Olaf to life, and he further broadened his filmography with an appearance in The Matrix Resurrections.

Offstage and offscreen, Harris leads a joyous personal life. He shares a beautiful and harmonious life with his husband, David Burtka, and their twins, Gideon and Harper. Together, they enjoy a family life filled with love and adventure, balancing his professional achievements with a fulfilling home life.

Alyson Hannigan as Lily Aldrin


Alyson Hannigan, beloved for her role as Lily Aldrin in How I Met Your Mother, has continued to showcase her diverse acting talents in various projects following the show’s conclusion. Her voice acting in the animated series Fancy Nancy has delighted younger audiences, while her role in the live-action film adaptation of Kim Possible demonstrated her adaptability to different genres. In 2023, Hannigan also proved her dancing prowess on Dancing with the Stars, impressively finishing in fifth place.

Beyond her professional life, Hannigan enjoys a fulfilling personal life. She shares a loving home with her husband, Alexis Denisof, whom she met on the set of Buffy the Vampire Slayer, and their two daughters. This harmonious blend of professional success and personal happiness highlights Hannigan’s ability to balance her career in entertainment with her family commitments.

Jason Segel as Marshall Eriksen


Jason Segel, remembered fondly as the endearing Marshall Eriksen from How I Met Your Mother, has continued to diversify his acting portfolio with an impressive mix of comedic and dramatic roles. His post-HIMYM career highlights include starring in the emotionally charged film Our Friend and portraying Paul Westhead in the HBO series Winning Time: The Rise of the Lakers Dynasty, a role that showcased his dramatic chops. Segel also received critical acclaim for his role in the Apple TV+ series Shrinking, earning an Emmy nomination and reaffirming his status as a versatile and compelling actor.

On a personal note, Segel has found happiness in a new relationship with dancer Kayla Radomski, after his previous long-term relationship. This new chapter in his life adds a joyful dimension to his evolving narrative, paralleling the depth and growth he continues to bring to his professional work.

Cristin Milioti as Tracy McConnell


Cristin Milioti, who captivated audiences as the elusive Tracy McConnell in How I Met Your Mother, has continued to shine in a variety of compelling roles across film and television. Milioti’s talents have been showcased in critically acclaimed series such as Fargo and The Mindy Project, where her unique blend of charisma and emotional depth brought her characters to life. Her standout performance in the hit film Palm Springs, opposite Andy Samberg, garnered particular praise for its originality and her dynamic acting.

Building on her momentum, Milioti is poised to make a significant impact with her upcoming lead role in the Max series The Penguin, which is expected to further cement her status as a rising star in Hollywood. Milioti’s career trajectory continues to ascend as she chooses diverse and challenging projects that highlight her versatility and magnetic screen presence.

Lyndsy Fonseca as Penny Mosby


Lyndsy Fonseca, known for her role as Ted’s daughter Penny in How I Met Your Mother, has successfully expanded her acting repertoire with a variety of significant roles in both television and film. Notably, her performances in series like Agent Carter and 9-1-1: Lone Star have showcased her ability to adapt to different genres, from action-packed sequences to intense drama, earning her praise for her versatility and depth as an actress.

Off the screen, Fonseca’s life is deeply fulfilling, highlighted by her marriage to Nikita co-star Noah Bean. Together, they have two daughters, enriching Fonseca’s off-camera world with family joy and stability. This balance between her professional accomplishments and personal happiness exemplifies Fonseca’s ability to manage a dynamic acting career while maintaining a grounded and nurturing home life, serving as an inspiration to many of her peers and fans.

David Henrie as Luke Mosby


David Henrie, fondly remembered as Luke Mosby from How I Met Your Mother, has impressively broadened his professional horizon beyond acting into directing. His directorial debut, This Is the Year, notably produced by Selena Gomez, marked a significant milestone in his career, showcasing his storytelling capabilities and creative vision. Henrie’s ambition continues to drive his career forward as he prepares to portray Ronald Reagan in the upcoming biopic Reagan.

Additionally, his commitment to his early roots is evident in his involvement in the Wizards of Waverly Place revival, reconnecting with his initial fan base and renewing his beloved character. On the personal front, Henrie enjoys a fulfilling family life, married to Maria Cahill. Together, they have three children, adding depth and joy to his off-screen life. Henrie’s journey from a child actor to a multifaceted industry professional demonstrates his growth and adaptability within the entertainment sphere.

As we look back on the careers of the How I Met Your Mother cast, it’s evident that each has carved a unique path in the entertainment world, staying relevant and successful in their respective endeavors. Their journeys remind us that while the show has ended, its impact and the evolution of its beloved stars continue to captivate and inspire audiences worldwide.
