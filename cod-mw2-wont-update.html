COD Warzone &amp; MW2 Won't Update Season 5

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, March 5, 2024

If Call of Duty Warzone 2 and Modern Warfare 2 won’t update in Season 5, you are not alone. The penultimate Call of Duty Modern Warfare 2 season has arrived stacked with new content. However, not everyone is able to play immediately as players are reporting various issues with updating the game. Here’s how to update COD MW2 and Warzone 2 to Season 5 on PS5, Xbox Series X/S, Xbox One, PS4, and PC.

How to Fix MW2 and Warzone 2 Won’t Update in Season 5

One of the most common issues at the start of every new season in Call of Duty games is the fact that the game simply refuses to update properly. Players know that the new season is here, but the game doesn’t show any updates. As a result, these players cannot play the game and enjoy the new seasonal content. Are you one of those players? Luckily for you, this is a very simple issue, and all you need to do is to take a few simple steps.

It goes without saying, but we must mention even the most obvious things – make sure that your internet is up and running and that Modern Warfare isn’t being blocked by your antivirus and firewall apps. If that’s all sorted out, here are other steps to fix the MW2 Won’t Update issue:

  • Restart the game.
  • Restart you PC or Console.
  • Power cycle your console to delete the system cache
    • Turn off your console completely.
    • Unplug the power cord.
    • Leave it at least two minutes.
    • Plug the power cord back and turn the console on.
  • Finally, if none of the solutions above helps, delete the game completely and install it from scratch.

So there you have it, one of these solutions should help you to fix the “COD Warzone & MW2 Won’t Update” error in Season 5.
