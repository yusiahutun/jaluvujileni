Ridgefield's Jesse Lee Church Seeks Sellers For Tag Sale

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Saturday, June 29, 2024

The tag sale will be at Jesse Lee Memorial United Methodist Church, 207 Main St.

For those interested in selling their items, features of the tag sale include:
  • It will occur on the driveway in front of the sanctuary, visible to those walking, running, biking and driving by on Main Street.
  • The church will handle the advertising and publicity.
  • It will be made up of Individual tag sales comprised into one.
  • It will be adjacent to the Jesse Lee Pumpkin Patch, which will be up and running and drawing people to the church campus. 
  • If it’s raining, it will move inside to Martin Hall (the lower level of the church’s sanctuary building). 
  • Cost to reserve a space is $15 for Jesse Lee members or $20 for non-members. 
  • Proceeds from the table spaces will support Jesse Lee Youth’s planned mission trip to help build a school in Belize next summer. 
  • After paying for their space in the event, sellers keep all revenues from their item sales. 

Deadline to reserve a space is Sunday, Sept. 20. 

To reserve a spot or for more information, call the church office at (203) 438-8791.
