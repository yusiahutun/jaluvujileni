Where was Hiba Abouk born? Ethnicity, Nationality, Family, Education

Hiba Abouk, who was born on October 30, 1986 in Madrid, Spain, is a Spanish national. Hiba Aboukhris Benslimane is the actress’s given name. Her mother is Tunisian and her father is Libyan, so she is of mixed ancestry. Her parents had previously moved to Spain from Tunisia. She is the fourth and youngest of four siblings.

Hiba will turn 36 years old in 2022 due to her date of birth. She is a practicing Muslim. Hiba was educated according to Islamic principles. She attended the French Lyceum in Madrid until she was 18 years old. She later studied Arabic philology and graduated in theater from RESAD.
